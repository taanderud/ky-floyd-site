1. ISAAC1 LITTLE was born Abt 1755, and died December 1823 in KY. He married WINNEY.   Notes In the 1773 Delinquent Tax Lists of Fincastle County, VA, it lists Isaac Little, Patrick Johnson, and Ephraim Osborn, all are ancesters of Jim Little. I found an Isaac Little in Russell County, VA in 1790 in the list of Personal Property. In 1810 Russell County, VA, I found William and Sarah Little listed. Isaac Little's Will was probated in Court Jan. 1824 in Pike County, KY, so I am assuming he died the month before. James Roberts was the administrator of Isaac's Will, even though Isaac seemed to have children and his wife in the area, although they may have been gone at the time. He didn't mention any names in his Will, just said wife and children. Peter Little of Clay and Wolfe County, KY is listed in the 1820 Floyd County, KY census with Isaac, James W., and William Little, so it is a possibility that he could be a relative someway.
