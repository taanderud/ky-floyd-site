James and Tina Johnson were married in 1923.  They had one child (Arlen Johnson) (It should be noted that on Arlen's birth certificate the name, Forrest Johnson is crossed out, and the name Arlen Johnson written above it.).

Arlen was born on Jun. 26, 1925-(He told my mother-(Alpha Jean Johnson that he was born in 1919).        James Johnson died in Jan. of 1926.  He was reported to have been shot by Tina's brother-(Eli / not confirmed). The date of Tina's marriage to John Smith has not been confirmed. It probably occurred about 1927/1928.  No children has been confirmed for this marriage. The attached picture has written on the back-(James and Tina / Sept. 23, 1923)
