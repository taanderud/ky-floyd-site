In Memory Of Kathy Hamilton



Kathleen Ann Koester Hamilton, 55, of Clinton, Michigan, passed from this earthly life, to her eternal home July 14, 2012 at University of Michigan hospital in Adrian, MI.

She was born in Wyandotte, MI, on October 29, 1956 to the late Bernard and Dollie Howell Koester. She was married to Timothy L. Hamilton, formerly of Floyd County, KY.

In addition to her husband, she is survived by one daughter, Elizabeth (Scott) Kurowicki of Manchester, MI, one son, Timothy C. (Anna) Hamilton of Adrian, MI, and two grandsons, Tyler Kurowicki and Jacob Kurowicki. Funeral

Services were held July 17, 2012 at Handler Funeral Home in Clinton, MI. with Pastor Tom Hawkins of New Song Community Church officiating. Burial was in Riverside Cemetery, Clinton, MI.

Kathy was involved in many activities in her home town of Clinton. She loved making baskets, quilts, and many other crafts, which she sold at festivals. Her hands were always busy.

Kathy also compiled several books of her research in genealogy and inventory of cemeteries in Floyd County.

She is greatly missed by all who knew her.

Note: KYKinFolks appreciates her years of dedication and work for future Floyd County family researchers.
