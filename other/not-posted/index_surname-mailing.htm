How to Subscribe and Unsubscribe using your browser

It's easy -- just click on the list you want. When the "mail-to" window pops up, write just the single word "subscribe" in the body of the message. That's all. Do the same to unsubscribe, just type "unsubscribe" in the body of the message. Then send.
