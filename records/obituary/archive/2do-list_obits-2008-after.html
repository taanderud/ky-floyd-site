MOORE, Arnold, 101, of Cheyenne died Jan. 14 at Kindred Transitional Care. He was born Sept. 19, 1913, in Floyd County, KY, a son of Harry and Fannie (Stumbo) Moore. Survivors include his wife, Della; two daughters, Shirley Coupens and Linda Hollings, both of Cheyenne; nine grandchildren and 20 great-grandchildren. He was preceded in death by his parents; three sons, Bernie, Jimmy and Kelvin Moore; nine brothers, Ballard, Russell, Ralph, Clyde, Edgle, Ranold, Girdle, Ivel and Rufus Moore; and a sister, Opal. Cremation has taken place at Cheyenne Memorial Gardens under the direction of Wiederspahn-Radomsky Chapel. Submitted By: Linda Moore Hollings
