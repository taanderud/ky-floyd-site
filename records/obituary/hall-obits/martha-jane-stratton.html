Martha Jane Stratton
Born: 09/04/1920
Died: 09/30/2004
Visitation: AFTER 6:00 P.M. FRIDAY AT THE FUNERAL HOME.
Funeral Service: Saturday, October 2, 2004 at 2:00 P.M. in the Hall FUneral Home Chapel, with Estil Reed officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens at Ivel, Ky.
 

MARTHA JANE STRATTON, AGE 84, OF CAMPBELLBURG, KY., FORMERLY OF IVEL, KY., WIDOW OF CLAYTON STRATTON, PASSED AWAY THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 30, 2004 IN THE UNIVERSITY HOSPITAL, LOUISVILLE, KY. SHE WAS BORN SEPTEMBER 4, 1920 IN ALLEN, KY., THE DAUGHTER OF THE LATE FRANK CANTERBURY AND CHRISTIE WOODS CANTERBURY. MRS. STRATTON ATTENDED BOTH BETSY LAYNE FREEWILL BAPTIST CHURCH AND BUCKNER BAPTIST CHURCH, BUCKNER, KY.

SURVIVORS INCLUDE ONE SON, JAMES STRATTON, HAGER HILL, KY., THREE DAUGHTERS, PATTY HORNBACK, CAMPBELLBURG, KY., ANNA TACKETT AND DINAH WARRIX BOTH OF NICHOLASVILLE, KY. ONE SISTER, MARY RUTH COLLINS, DRIFT, KY., 5 GRANDCHILDREN,MELISSA RAGLAND, CRAIG TACKETT, KRISTOL WARRIX, TRESHA MARTIN AND VALERIE MACE, 3 GREAT GRANDCHILDREN, BREANNA RAGLAND, KYLE TACKETT AND MACKENZIE TACKETT.

IN ADDITION TO HER HUSBAND AND PARENTS SHE WAS PRECEDED IN DEATH BY ON DAUGHTER, BESSIE SOWDER.
