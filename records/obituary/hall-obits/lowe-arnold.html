Mr. Arnold Lowe
Born: 1912-10-14
Died: 2004-05-17
Visitaton: After 12:00 Noon Wednesday, May 19, 2004 here at Hall Funeral Home
Funeral Service: 11:00 a.m. Thursday, May 20, 2004 here at Hall Funeral Home with Bobby Isaac & Dennis Love officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens, Ivel, Ky.
 

ARNOLD LOWE, AGE 91, OF EASTERN, KY, HUSBAND OF ALMA REFFITT LOWE, PASSED AWAY MONDAY, MAY 17, 2004, AT THE HIGHLANDS REGIONAL MEDICAL CENTER, PRESTONSURG, KENTUCKY. HE WAS BORN OCTOBER 14, 1912 IN PIKE COUNTY, KY, THE SON OF THE LATE PRILLIMAN ?PRILL? LOWE AND KANSAS BLACKBURN LOWE. MR. LOWE WAS A RETIRED PRODUCTION SUPERINTENDENT FOR COLUMBIA GAS, HE WAS OF THE CHRISTIAN FAITH, THE OLDEST CHARTER MEMBER OF FARM BUREAU, AN ADVISOR FOR THE 4H CLUB FOR 28 YEARS, AND A MEMBER OF THE BOARD OF THE FLOYD COUNTY ENTENSION SERVICES.

SURVIVORS INCLUDE HIS WIFE, ALMA, TWO SONS, DONALD (LOU) LAMAR LOWE, LARUEL, MS, ARNOLD BURGESS (MARGARET) LOWE, CANAL FULTON, OH, ONE DAUGHTER, CLAUDENA (KENNETH) HAMILTON, VIRGIE, KY, THREE SISTERS, ORTHA SCHEVER, BAKERSFIELD, CA,OLGA BLACKBURN, MT. STERLING, KY, DOROTHY MAYNARD, RACCOON, KY, EIGHT GRANDCHIDLREN, MARK LOWE, LAURENA LOWE, DENISE TAYLOR, STEPHANIE LOGAN, GLENN HAMILTON, SENDY NEWSOME, JESSICA LOWE, and DANIEL LOWE FIFTEEN GREAT GRANDCHILDREN, 1 GREAT-GREAT GRANDCHILD AND DAUGHTER IN LAW, JULIE LOWE.

IN ADDITION TO HIS PARENTS HE WAS PRECEDED IN DEATH BY ONE SON, PALMER KEITH LOWE AND ONE GRANDSON BENHAMIN THOMAS LOWE.
