Mr. Billy M. Allen
Born: 1930-03-27
Died: 2004-05-04
Visitaton: 6:00 PM, Wednesday, May 05
Funeral Service: 11:00 AM, Friday, May 07
Interment: Stewart Cemetery, Langley, Kentucky
 

BILLY M. ALLEN, AGE 74, OF LANGLEY, KY, HUSBAND OF DORA FRASURE ALLEN, PASSED AWAY TUESDAY, MAY 04, 2004, AT THE HIGHLANDS REGIONAL MEDICAL CENTER, PRESTONSBURG, KENTUCKY. HE WAS BORN MARCH 27, 1930 IN LANGLEY, KY, THE SON OF THE LATE U.S. ALLEN AND SARAH HYDEN ALLEN. MR. ALLEN WAS A RETIRED MECHANIC FOR HALBERT GARAGE AND A MEMBER OF THE UMWA.

 

 

 

SURVIVORS INCLUDE HIS WIFE DORA, ONE SON, WILLIAM K. ALLEN, MESA, AZ, TWO DAUGHTERS, EMMA JANE ALLEN, VERSAILLES, KY, SHEILA (MICHAEL) HALBHERR, INDIANAPOLIS, IN, TWO SISTERS, AGNES VAN HOOSE, LANGLEY, KY, JUANITA HAWKINS, SIDNEY, OH, FOUR GRANDCHILDREN, ERIC, GEOFFREY, STEPHANIE, AND MATTHEW HALBHERR.

 

 

IN ADDITION TO HIS PARENTS HE WAS PRECEDED IN DEATH BY ONE BROTHER, EDGEL ALLEN.
