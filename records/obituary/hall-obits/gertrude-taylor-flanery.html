Hall Funeral Home Records

 

Mrs. GERTRUDE TAYLOR FLANERY
Born: 03/10/1921
Died: 06/28/2004
Visitation: AFTER 5:00 P.M. THURSDAY AT THE FUNERAL HOME
Funeral Service: FRIDAY, JULY 2, 2004 AT 11:00 A.M. IN THE FUNERAL HOME CHAPEL WITH JOHNNY COLLINS OFFICIATING.
Interment: MARTIN CEMETERY AT MARTIN, KY.
 

GERTRUDE TAYLOR FLANERY, AGE 83, OF FT. WALTON BEACH, FL, FORMERLY OF MARTIN, KY, PASSED AWAY MONDAY, JUNE 28, 2004 AT HER RESIDENCE IN FLORDIA. SHE WAS BORN AT HUEYSVILLE, KY. ON MARCH 10, 1921, THE DAUGHTER OF THE LATE JAMES CASTLE AND AMERICA ROBINSON CASTLE.

SURVIVORS INCLUDE SIX SONS, TOMMY TAYLOR, WAYLAND, KY, ROGER TAYLOR OF TUSCON, ARIZONA, DON TAYLOR OF DE FUNIAK SPRINGS, FLORIDA, SCOTT TAYLOR OF JACKSON, MICHIGAN, DAN TAYLOR OF LESLIE, MICHIGAN AND LARRY TAYLOR OF JACKSON, MICHIGAN. ONE SISTER; BETTY MARKEY OF JACKSONVILLE, FLORIDA, 20 GRANDCHILDREN AND SEVERAL GREAT GRANDCHILDREN.

SHE WAS PRECEDED IN DEATH BY HER PARENTS, FIRST HUSBAND CICEROE TAYLOR, SECOND HUSBAND PHILLIP FLANERY AND ONE SON CHARLES TAYLOR
