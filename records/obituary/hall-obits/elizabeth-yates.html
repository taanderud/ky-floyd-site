Elizabeth Yates
Born: 03/01/1928
Died: 10/22/2004
Visitation: after 5:00 P.M. Saturday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Monday, October 25, 2004 at 11:00 A.M. in the Funeral Chapel with Ed Hurley officiating.
Interment: Lawson Cemetery at Honaker, Ky.

   

Elizabeth Yates age 74 of Honaker, Ky. passed away on Friday, October 22, 2004 in the U.K. Medical Center in Lexington, Ky. after a long illness. She was born March 1, 1928 in Robinson Creek, W.Va. the daughter of the late Estill and Oma Akers Coleman.

She was a homemaker and member of the Church of Christ.

She is survived by her husband, okie L. Yates, Three Sons: Danny L. yates of Wyandotte, Mich., Estill Yates and Terry yates both of Honaker, Ky. One Daughter: Joann Davet of Clayton, N.C. Three Brothers: Emmett Lawson and Roy Lawson of Harold, Ky. and Okie Lawson of Harold, Ky. Four Sisters: Maudie Hill of Harold, Ky., Reba Lawson, Shelia Lawson and Coyetta Lawson all of Honaker, Ky. 6 GHrandchildren and 2 Great Grandchildren.

Funeral Services: Monday, October 25, 2004 at 11:00 A.M. in the Hall Funeral Home Chapel at Martin, Ky. with Ed Hurley officating. Burial in the Lawson Cemetery at Honaker, Ky.

Under the professional care of Hall Funeral Home of Martin, Ky.
