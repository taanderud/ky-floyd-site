EDGAR HOWELL
Born: 12/13/1915
Died: 10/27/2004
Visitation: FROM 5 UNTIL 8:00 P.M. FRIDAY AT THE FUNERAL HOME
Funeral Service: SATURDAY, OCTOBER 30, 2004 AT 11:00 A.M. IN THE FUNERAL HOME CHAPEL WITH MIKE BLANTON OFFICIATING.
Interment: DAVIDSON MEMORIAL GARDENS AT IVEL, KENTUCKY
 

Edgar Howell, age 88 was born at Graynor, Ky. on December 13, 1915 the son of the late Levi and Dollie Martin Howell and passed away on Wednesday, October 27, 2004 in the Marion General Hospital in Marion, Ohio.

He was a self employed car dealer and a former Floyd COunty Magistrate.

Surviving are: The Wife, Jessie ARobbins Howell, One Son, james E. (Ruth) Howell of Marion, Ohio and One Daughter: Virginia (Doug) Hall of harold, Ky. One Brother, Archie Howell of Myrtle Beach, S.C. 6 Grandchildren and 12 Great Grandchildren.
