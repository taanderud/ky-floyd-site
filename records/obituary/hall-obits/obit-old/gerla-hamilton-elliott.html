GERLA HAMILTON ELLIOTT

Born: 12/19/1913
Died: 11/02/2004
Visitation: After 6:00 P.M. Wednesday, November 3, 2004 at the funeral home and after 10:30 A.M. Friday at the church.
Funeral Service: FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 5, 2004 AT 11:30 A.M. IN NTHE LITTLE ROSA OLD REGULAR BAPTIST CHURCH AT McDOWELL, KY. WITH MINISTERS OF THE CHURCH OFFICIATING.
Interment: Lucy Hall Cemetery at McDowell, Ky.
 

Gerla Hamilton Elliott, age 90, of Prestonsburg, Ky, formerly of McDowell, Ky, widow of Mines Elliott, passed away Wednesday, November 03, 2004, at the Highlands Regional Medical Center, Prestonsburg, Kentucky. She was born December 19 1913, in McDowell, Ky, the daughter of the late William Hamilton and Maudie Hall Hamilton. Mrs. Elliott was former employee of Industrial Rayon, Painesville, Ohio.

Survivors include one step-son, Bill Elliott, Circleville, Oh, one step-daughter, Launa Elliott, Ashtabula, Oh, two brothers, Homer Hamilton, Mcdowell, Ky, Sam Hamilton, Beech Grove, In, two sisters, Anna Moore and Ocie Moore Mitchell both of McDowell, Ky, several step-grandchildren, step-great grandchildren and nieces and nephews.
