Daniel Dexter Boggs

Born: 12/20/1946
Died: 07/07/2006
Visitation: after 6:00 P.M. Saturday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Monday, Jauly 10, 2006 at 12:00 Noon in the Funeral Home Chapel with Ronnie Spriggs officiating.
Interment: Gethsemane Gardens
 
Daniel Dexter Boggs, age 59, of East Point, Ky., passed away Friday, July 7, 2006, at the Pikeville Medical Center, Pikeville, Kentucky. He was born December 29, 1946 in East Point, Ky., the son of the late Edgar Boggs and Lou Ella Music Boggs. Mr. Boggs as a Coal Mining Supervisor, a U.S. Army, Vietnam War Veteran, a Member of the Hager Hill Freewill Baptist Church and a member of the Masonic Lodge.  Survivors include his companion, Phyllis Dotson, East Point, Ky.,a daughter Elizabeth (Neil) Ashley Boggs Capper, Clemson, Sc., two Step-children, Leann George and Kevin Dotson both of Prestonsburg, Ky., a brother, Ed Boggs, East Point, Ky., three sisters, Wanda Webb, East Point, Ky., Phyllis (LeMayne) Dingus, Martin, Ky., Elaine (J.P.) Short, Paintsville, Ky., six step-grandchildren, Emma George, Lucas George, Mason Holbrook, Austin Holbrook, Chase Dotson and Conor Dotson.   Preceding him in death were his parents and a sister Patsy Williams. Funeral services for Daniel Dexter Boggs will be conducted Monday, July 10, 2006, at 12:00 noon at The Hall Funeral Home Chapel, Martin, Ky., with Clergyman Ronnie Spriggs officiating. Burial will follow in Gethsemane Gardens, Prestonsburg, Ky., under the professional care of Hall Funeral Home, Martin, Kentucky. Visitation after 6:00 p.m., Saturday Hall Funeral Home.
