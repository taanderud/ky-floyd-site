EFFIE SHEPHERD CRAGER
Born: 07/24/1912
Died: 08/17/2004
Visitation: after 6:00 P.M. Wednesday at the funeral home
Funeral Service: friday, August 20, 2004 at 11:00 A.M. in the Chapel with Sterlin Bolen, Carlos Beverly and Dolvin Ratliff officiating.
Interment: Crager Family Cemtery at Hueysville, Ky.
 

Effie Grager, age 92 of Hueysville, Kentucky, entered eternal rest on Tuesday Evening, August 17, 2004 in the Riverview Health Care Center in Prestonsburg, Kentucky. She was born on July 24, 1912 at Magoffin Co., being the daughter of the late Willie and Martha Howard Shepherd.

She was a homemaker and a member of the Salt Lick Community Church at Hueysville, Ky.

Effie is survived by, Four Sons: Norman Crager, Vernon Crager and Cletis Crager both of Hueysville, Ky. and Herman Crager of Corunna, Indiana. Two Daughters: Ruth Wallace of Hueysville, Ky. and Marie Hicks of Kendallville, Indiana, Three Sisters: Ruby Gayheart of Prestonsburg, Ky., Marie Conley and Sally Salisbury both of Hueysville, Ky. Also surviving are, 11 Grandchildren, 20 Great Grandchildren and 1 Great-Great Grandchild.

She was preceded in death by, Her Parents Willie and Martha, Husband, Jimmy Crager, Son: Estill Crager, Brother: John Estill Shepherd, 2 Grandchildren and 3 Great-Great Grandchildren.

Funeral Services: Friday, August 20, 2004 at 11:00 A.M. in the Hall Funeral Home Chapel in Martin, Ky. with Sterlin Bolen, Carlos Beverly and Dolvin Ratliff officiating. She will be laid to rest in the Crager Family Cemetery on Raccoon Creek at Hueysville, Ky. Under the professional care of Hall Funeral Home of Martin, Ky.
