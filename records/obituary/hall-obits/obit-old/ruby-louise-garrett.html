RUBY LOUISE GARRETT
Born: 08/29/1924
Died: 08/07/2006
Visitation: after 5:00 P.M. Wednesday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Friday, August 11, 2006 at 11:00 A.M. Betsy Layne Freewill Baptist Church with Tracy Patton officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens
 
Ruby Louise Garrett, age 81, of Stanville, Ky., widow of Leonard Garrett, passed away Monday, August 07, 2006 at the Pikeville Medical Center, Pikeville, Kentucky. She was born August 29, 1924 the daughter of the late Carl L. Senters and Ethel Miller Senters. Mrs. Garrett was a homemaker and a member of The Betsy Layne Freewill Baptist Church, Betsy Layne, Kentucky.

Survivors include a daughter, Ethel Mae(Corbett) Hurt, Birmingham, Al., five brothers, Willis Senters, Loraine, Oh., Bobby Senters, Stanville, Ky., Irvin Senters, Farmington, Mi., Fred Senters and Buddy Boy Senters both of Stanville, Ky., three sisters, Norma Jean Letrounae,Linton, Mi., Clotine Fuller, Detroit, Mi., Georgene Iricks, Marion, Oh., seven grandchildren, Ryan Hurt, Scott Hurt, Leslie Hurt, Corbett Hurt, III., Gina Wilson, Kimberly Davis and Rodney Garrett.
