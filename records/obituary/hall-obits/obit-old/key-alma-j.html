Mrs. Alma J. Key
Born: 1916-06-18
Died: 2004-05-21
Visitaton: after 6:00 P.M. Saturday, May 22, 2004 at the Funeral Home, with Eastern Star Services at 7:00 P.M. Sunday evening.
Funeral Service: HALL FUNERAL HOME CHAPEL, Monday, May 24, 2004 at 11:00 A.M. with Garfield Potter and Roy Harlow officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens at Ivel, Ky.
 

 

Alma J. Key, age 87 of Martin, Ky. passed away on Friday, May 21, 2004 in Our Lady of the Way Hospital in Martin, Ky. She was born on June 18, 1916 at Bull Creek, being the daughter of the late William G. And Frances Lafferty Jarrell.

 

She was a homemaker and a member of the Eastern Star and the Martin Methodist Church.

 

Survivors include: Husband, J.R. Key of Martin, Ky., Sons: Bill Griffith and Rudolph Griffith both of Prestonsburg, Ky. and Robert Griffith of Martin, Ky. Two Daughters: Brenda Settles of Danville, Ky. and Susan Key of Martin, Ky. Sister: Helen Jarrell of Prestonsburg, Ky., 14 Grandchildren, 17 Great Grandchildren.
