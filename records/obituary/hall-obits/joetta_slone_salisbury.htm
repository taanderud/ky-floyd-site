JOETTA SLONE SALISBURY

Born: 11/30/1957
Died: 06/24/2004
Visitation: after 5:00 P.M. Saturday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Monday, June 28, 2004 at 2:00 P.M. in the Funeral Chapel with Old Regular Baptist Ministers officiating.
Interment: Newman Cemetery at Hi Hat, Ky.

JOETTA SLONE SALISBURY, AGE 46 OF HI HAT, KY. WAS BORN ON NOVEMBER 30, 1957 AT PAINTSVILLE, KY. BEING THE DAUGHTER OF JOYCE RAY SLONE OF HI HAT, KY. AND THE LATE VIRGIL SLONE. SHE DEPARTED THIS LIFE ON THURSDAY NIGHT, JUNE 24, 2004 IN THE U.K. MEDICAL CENTER IN LEXINGTON, KY.

SHE WAS EMPLOYED WITH THE FLOYD COUNTY COURT CLERKS OFFICE.

SURVIVING ARE: THE MOTHER, JOYCE SLONE OF HI HAT, KY., ONE SON; DANIEL BRYAN RUSSELL SALISBURY OF RIVER, KY. AND ONE DAUGHTER; TIA LASHAE SALISBURY OF MINNIE, KY. TWO BROTHERS; VERNON SLONE OF LOTTS CREEK, KY. AND VIRGIL SLONE, JR. OF HI HAT, KY. THREE SISTERS; JOAN SLONE AND DEBBIE SLONE BOTH OF HI HAT, KY. AND HENRIETTA GIBSON OF LIGON, KY.
