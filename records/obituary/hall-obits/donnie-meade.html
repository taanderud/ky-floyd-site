Donnie Meade
Born: 05/12/1947
Died: 12/03/2005
Visitation: 3:00 P.M. until 7:00 P.M. Sunday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Sunday, December 4, 2005 at 7:00 P.M. in the Hall Funeral Home Chapel with Rev. Roy Harlow officiating.
Interment:
 

Donnie Meade age 58 of Drift, Ky. passed away on Sunday, December 3, 2005 at his residence after a sudden illness. He was born May 12, 1947 at Martin, Kentucky the son of Betty Showers Meade of Langley, Ky. and the late Donald Meade.

He was retired from the Kentucky State Department of Transportation.

He is survived by is mother, Betty Wood Showers Meade of Langley, Ky. and One Sister, Toni Ann Meade Bailey of Langley, Ky.

Funeral Services: Sunday, December 4, 2005 at 7:00 P.M. in the Hall Funeral Home Chapel at Martin, Ky. with Rev. Roy Harlow officiating. Visitation at the Funeral Home from 3:00 P.M. until 7:00 P.M. Sunday at the Chapel.
