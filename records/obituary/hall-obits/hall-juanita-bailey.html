MRS. HALL WAS BORN JANUARY 22ND, 1918 IN RUSH, KENTUCKY. SHE WAS THE DAUGHTER OF THE LATE PIERCE AND MARY ANN BAILEY. HER HUSBAND, WILLIAM R. HALL, WHO PASSED AWAY OCTOBER 27TH, 1989. SHE ACCEPTED CHRIST AS HER SAVIOR ON AUGUST 19TH, 2002 IN THE PRESENCE OF BETH, HER GRANDDAUGHTER.

SHE WAS A HOMEMAKER AND WORKED VARIOUS JOBS IN WHEELWRIGHT AND PRESTONBURG.
