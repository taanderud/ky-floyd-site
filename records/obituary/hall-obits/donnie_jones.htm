Donnie Jones
Born: 10/08/1948
Died: 10/08/2004
Visitation: From 5:00 to 9:00 p.m. Monday here at Hall Funeral Home.
Funeral Service: 1:00 p.m. Tuesday, October 12, 2004 here at Hall Funeral Home with Shannon McKinney officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens, Ivel, Ky.
 

Donnie Jones, age 56, of Stanville, Ky., passed away Friday, October 8, 2004 in the residence of his daughter in Danville, IN. He was born April 21, 1948 in McDowell (Dony) Ky., the son of the late Earl Jones and Armina Stumbo Jones. Mr. Jones was U.S. Army Veteran of the Vietnam War.

Mr. Jones is survived by three Daughters, Donna Sue (Randy) Jones, Danville, IN., Lynelle Marie (David) Cullen, Grand View, MO., Melissa Elkins, Grethel, Ky., Four Brothers, Edsel Ray (Pat) Jones, Pekin, ILL., Petral Gene (Laura) Jones Fredricksburg, VA., Herman (Sally) Jones Tram, Ky. and James Ricketts of Partlow, VA., two Sisters, Glenda Donohue, Partlow, VA., Rhonda Lynn (Paul) Williams, Kendallville, IN., 3 Grandchildren, Brandon, Jesi and Kody

In addition to his parents he was preceded in death by one brother, Sherman Jones, one Sister, Juanita Jones Puterbaugh.
