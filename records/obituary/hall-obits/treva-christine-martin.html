Hall Funeral Home Records

Mrs. Treva Christine Martin

Born: 03/30/1926
Died: 07/06/2004
Visitation: 4 P.M., Friday, July 9
Funeral Service: 11 A.M., Saturday, July 10
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens, Ivel, Ky

TREVA CHRISTINE MARTIN, AGE 78, OF PRESTONSBURG, KY, WIDOW OF KERMIT MARTIN, PASSED AWAY ON TUESDAY, JULY 6, 2004, AT THE CLEVLAND CLINIC, CLEVLAND, OHIO. SHE WAS BORN MARCH 30, 1926, THE DAUGHTER OF THE LATE VARNON & SADIE OSBORNE RATLIFF. MRS MARTIN WAS A HOUSEWIFE AND A MEMBER OF THE PLESANT HOME BAPTIST CHURCH.

MRS. MARTIN IS SURVIVED BY TWO SONS; MICHAEL MARTIN, OF MARYSVILLE, PA & KEN MARTIN OF MARTINSBURG, WV, ONE DAUGHTER; GLORIA JEAN WIANT, OF STEPHENS CITY, VA, THREE SISTERS; DIXIE HYDEN, OF MARTIN, KY, WANDA CAUDILL, OF ALLEN, KY, & BARBARA TACKETT, OF ALLEN, KY, SIX GRANDCHILDREN AND TWO GREAT GRANDCHILDREN

IN ADDITION TO HER PARENTS, MRS. MARTIN WAS PRECEEDED IN DEATH BY ONE SISTER DORIS MARTIN.
