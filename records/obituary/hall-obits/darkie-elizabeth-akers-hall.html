Darkie Elizabeth Akers Hall
Born: 04/18/1915
Died: 01/07/2006
Visitation: After 3:00 p.m. Sunday, January 8, 2006 at the Pilgrim Home Regular Baptist Church, Grethel, Ky.
Funeral Service: 11:00 a.m. Tuesday, January 10, 2005 at the Pilgrim Home Regular Baptist Church, Grethel, Ky. with Regular Baptist Ministers officiating.
Interment: Hall Family Cemetery, Grethel, Ky.
 

Darkie Elizabeth Akers Hall, age 90, of Grethel, Ky., wife of the late Joe J. Hall, passed away on Saturday, January 7, 2006 at her residence in Grethel, Ky. She was born April 18, 1915 in Grethel, Ky., the daughter of the late Kenas Akers and Judy Hamilton Akers. Mrs Hall was a member of Pilgrim Home Regular Baptist Church, Grethel, Ky. and a homemaker.

Survivors include her six sons, John M. Hall and Andy Steve Hall both of Harold, Ky., Kenas Hall, Stanford, Ky., Joe Hall Jr., James D. Hall and Donald Ray Hall all of Grethel, Ky., 14 grandchildren, 17 great grandchildren and 2 great great grandchildren

In addition to her husband and parents she was preceded in death by one son, Sherman Lee Hall, four brothers, Andy Akers, Bert Akers, Sie Akers and William Akers.

Funeral services for Darkie Elizabeth Akers Hall will be conducted Tuesday, January 10, 2006, at 11:00 a.m., at the Pilgrim Home Regular Baptist Church, Grethel, Ky, with Regular Baptist Ministers officiating. Burial will follow in the Hall Family Cemetery, Grethel, Ky., under the professional care of the Hall Funeral Home, Martin, Kentucky. Visitation is after 3:00 p.m., Sunday here at the Church.
