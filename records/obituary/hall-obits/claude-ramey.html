HE IS SURVIVED BY: HIS COMPANION, DEBORAH NEELEY, EX-WIFE: CAROL RAMEY. SONS: DEREK RAY RAMEY PRATER, JONATHAN CLAUDE RAMEY AND ADAM CURTIS RAMEY. BROTHERS: BRADFORD RAMEY AND RAY RAMEY. SISTERS: EDNA BLEVINS, EVIE ZOLMAN, SHIRLEY ROSE, FAYE HOWARD, CLAUSIE COX, BRENDA PATTON AND BARBARA STANFORD.
