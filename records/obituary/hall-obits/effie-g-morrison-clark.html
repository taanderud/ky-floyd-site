EFFIE G. MORRISON CLARK
Born: 08/29/1912
Died: 01/02/2006
Visitation: After 1:00 P.M. Tuesday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Wednesday, January 4, 2006 at 2:00 P.M. in the Hall FUneral Chapel with Montie Fugate and Dennis Love officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens

   

Effie G. Morrison Clark, age 93 of Harold, Ky. the wife of George Edward Clark passed away on Monday, January 2, 2006 at her residence following a extended illness. Effie was born at Denton, Ky. on August 29, 1912 and was the daughter of the late William H. and Ida Mae Hutchinson Morrison.

Beside her parents she was preceded in death by four brothers and their wives, Herman (Etta), Clarence (Hager), Herchelle (Dorothy) and Orville (Sarah) Morrison and a son-in-law, Randall Coggins.

Mrs. Clark was a retired registered nurse with nearly 35 years of experience, a charter member of the Disabled American Veterans Auxillary Chapter 169 of Betsy Layne, and a member of the Vogel-Dday United Methodist Church for over 47 years.

Mrs. Clark is survived by her husband of 65 years, George Edward Clark, One Son, George Edward (Rosemary) Clark II and One Daughter, Billie Joyce Coggins. Three Grandchildren: George Edward (Samantha) Clark III and Brian (Michelle) White both of Harold, Ky., and Terra Lynn (Ranjan) Maitra of Gastonia, North Carolina Great Grandchildren: Steven Edward Clark, Rebecca Clark and Layne Alexandra Maitra, Step Great Grandchildren: Justin and Kayla Kidd, Sister in Law: Doris Clark of Allen, Kentucky. Numerous Nieces and Nephews.

Funeral Services: Wednesday, January 4, 2006 at 2:00 P.M. in the Hall Funeral Home Chapel at Martin, Ky. with Montie Fugate and Dennis Love officiating. Burial in the Davidson Memorial Gardens at Ivel, Ky. Under the Professional Care of Hall Funeral Home of Martin, Ky.

Pallbearers: George Ed "Edward" Clark III, Ronnie Leedy, David Ray York, Justin Kidd, Steve Morrison and Ronnie Justice.
