Hall Funeral Home Records

John Graham Ratliff

Born: 05/16/1927
Died: 06/19/2004
Visitation: AFTER 6:00 P.M. SATURDAY AT THE CHAPEL
Funeral Service: Monday, June 21, 2004 at 11:00 A.M. in the Funeral Chapel, with Church of Christ Ministers officiating.
Interment: DAVIDSON MEMORIAL GARDENS AT IVEL, KY.

John Graham Ratliff, age 77 of Stanville, Kentucky passed away on Saturday, June 19, 2004 in the Park View Manor Nursing Home in Pikeville, Kentucky. He was born on May 16, 1927 in Floyd County, being the son of the late Jim and Sophia Morrison Ratliff.

He was a retired welder for Diamond Coal Co. and a member of the Church of Christ.

His first wife preceded him in death: Betty Ann Ratliff and daughter; Joann Ratliff.

He is survived by; his wife, Vern Edith Meade Ratliff of Stanville, Ky. Step Sons; Joe Schmidt of Idaho, John Meade of Prestonsburg, Ky., Randall Meade of Lancaster, Ky. Jeffrey Meade of Printer, Ky., Luther Ray Meade of Printer, Ky., George Meade, Jr. of Etna Green, Indiana and Carl Meade of McDowell, Ky., Step Daughters; Tabitha Carlington of Taylor Michigan and Beatrice Collins of McDowell, Ky. One Half - Brother, Rutherford James of Prestonsburg, Ky., One Sister, Joetta Ratliff of Pikeville, KY. 27 Step Grandchildren, 10 Step Great Grandchildren.

ADDITIONALLY, HE WAS PRECEDED IN DEATH BY HIS FIRST WIFE, BETTY ANN RATLIFF, ONE DAUGHTER, JOANN RATLIFF, SIX HALF BROTHERS, BALLARD, EDGAR, STERLIE, SHIRLEY, BURLEY RATLIFF, AND CHESTER JAMES, FIVE SISTERS, OVA RATLIFF, LIZZIE RATLIFF BLACKBURN, BYRD RATLIFF BLACKBURN, PAULINE "COON" RATLIFF McCOY AND ZETTA RATLIFF MORRISON
