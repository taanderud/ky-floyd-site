Sylvia Sellards Hall
Born: 11/15/1920
Died: 11/25/2004
Visitation: After 6:00 P.M. Friday, 11/26/04 at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Sunday, November 28, 2004 at 12:30 P.M. in the Hall Funeral Chapel with Arnold Turner, Jr. and Doug Lewis Officiating.
Interment: Family Cemetery at Banner, Kentucky
 

Sylvia Sellards Hall, age 84, of Staville, Ky, wife of the late Fred C. Hall, passed away Thursday, November 25, 2004, at the Pikeville Methodist Hospital, Pikeville, Kentucky. She was born November 15, 1920, at Banner, Ky, the daughter of the late Jack Sellards and Nancy Akers Sellards. Mrs. Hall was a homemaker and a member of the First Church of Christ Scientist.

Survivors include two daughters, Karen Hall Hamilton and Holly Denise Hall both of Stanville, Ky, one brother, Jack Buford Sellards, Corbin, Ky, three sisters, Aileen Hall, Betsy Layne, Ky, Jewel Conn, Harold, Ky, Georgean Cecil, Stanville, Ky, and three grandchildren, Amanda Hamilton, Paige Duncan and Drew Duncan.

She was preceded in death by her husband, parents, two brothers, Edward Grahan Sellards, David Estill Sellards, and one sister, Elsie Estep.
