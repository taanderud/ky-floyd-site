MIKE WIREMAN
Born: 12/24/1967
Died: 03/26/2005
Visitation: AFTER 5:00 P.M. MONDAY AT THE FUNERAL HOME.
Funeral Service: WEDNESDAY, MARCH 30, 2005 AT 1:00 P.M. IN THE FUNERAL HOME CHAPEL WITH ROGER BOLEN, BILL TUSSEY, JR. AND EARL MILLER.
Interment: SHEPHERD CEMETERY AT SALT LICK CREEK - HUEYSVILLE.

    Mike Wireman age 37 of Hueysville, Ky. passed away on Saturday evening, March 26, 2005 at his residence. He was born December 24, 1967 in Columbia City, Indiana the son of Ondie and Kathleen Whittaker Wireman.

He was a member of the National Guard and attended the Upper Quicksand Free Pentecostal Church at Hueysville.

Survivng are: The Parents, Ondie and Kathleen Wireman of Hueysville, Ky. One Brother, Chris Wireman of Hueysville, Ky. Two Sisters, Athlene (WIlliam) Patton of Versailles, Ky. and Kim (Brian) Tussey of Kendalville, Indiana. Nephews: Jason Patton, Daniel Tussey, Andrew Tussey, Neice: Hannah Patton. 8 Aunts, 4 Uncles and a host of Cousins.

Funeral Services: Wednesday, march 30, 2005 at 1:00 P.M. in the Hall FUneral Chapel at Martin, Ky. with Roger Bolen, Earl Miller and Bill Tussey, Jr. officiating. Burial in the Shepherd Cemetery at Hueysville, Ky. Under the direction of HALL FUNERAL HOME.
