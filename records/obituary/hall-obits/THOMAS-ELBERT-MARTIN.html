THOMAS ELBERT MARTIN
Born: 02/25/1941
Died: 05/29/2006
Visitation: after 5:00 P.M. Tuesday at the Funeral Home. Services Wednesday night at 7:00 P.M.
Funeral Service: Thursday, June 1, 2006 at 11:00 A.M. in Hall Funeral Home with Regular Baptist Ministers officiating.
Interment: Martin Cemetery at Eastern, Ky.
 
Thomas Elbert Martin age 65 of Turkey Creek Rd. Langley, Ky. passed from this life on Monday, May 29, 2006 in Highlands Regional Medical Center after a lengthy illness. He was born at Eastern, Ky. on February 25, 1941 the son of the late Garland and Nora Pratt Martin.

He was a retired maintenance mechanic for Columbia Gas Transmission Co. and a Veteran of the United States Air Force.

He is survived by: His wife, Evelene Moore Martin, Two Sons, John Thomas Martin of Versailles, Ky. and Franklin Lee Martin of Langley, Ky. Two Brothers, Chalmer Martin of Stanton, Ky. and Gaylord Martin of Langley, Ky. Four Sisters, Daphne K. Moore of Langley, Ky., Glema Gay Moore of Langley, Ky., Daina Martin Purvis of Eminence, Ky. and June Claudette Martin of Garrett, Ky. Three Grandchildren, Ashlyn Grace Martin, Keaton Thomas Martin and Max Andrew Martin.

Other than the parents, he was preceded in death by a sister, Joan Tonda Duff.

Funeral Services: Thursday, June 1, 2006 at 11:00 A.M. in the Hall Funeral Home Chapel at Martin, Ky. with Regular Baptist Ministers officiating. Burial in the Martin Cemetery at Eastern, Ky. Under the professional care of Hall Funeral Home.
