Randall Gene "Butter Bean" Coggins
Born: 06/30/1950
Died: 10/06/2004
Visitation: After 2:00 p.m. Saturday, October 9, 2004 in the Hall Funeral Home.
Funeral Service: 2:00 p.m. Sunday, October 10, 2004 in the Hall Funeral Home with Levi Coleman officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens, Ivel, Ky.
 

Randall Gene �Butter Bean� Coggins, age 54of Harold, Ky., left his earthly home to meet his Savior on Wednesday, October 6, 2004 at the University of Kentucky Hospital at 2:30 p.m. after sudden illness.

Mr. Coggins was born June 30, 1950 in Pikeville, Ky.. He was the son of the late Raymond R. Coggins of Shelbiana, Ky., and the late Nell Ruth Justice Coggins Bohanan of Geronimo, OK. He was preceded in death by both sets of grandparents, Bradley and Dill Justice f Shelbiana, Ky. and Oscar and Alice Coggins of Norh Carolina.

He was employed by NIK Construction Company as a heavy Equipment operator.

Mr. Coggins is survived by his wife, Billie Joyce Clark White Coggins and step son Jerry Brian White both of Harold, Ky., two sisters, Sylvia, (J.W.) Thompson of Hale, MI., abd Dorothy (Kenneth) Corder of Grayson, Ky., one brother, Todd Edward Coggins of Henderson, NV., his Step Father, Bert Bohanan of Geromino, OK., one nephew, nine nieces, seven great nephews and nine great nieces. He was a member of the United Steelworkers of America and was of the Methodist Faith. He was a loving Husband, Father, Son, Brother, Uncle, Nephew and friend. He wiil be missed by all who knew him. He never realized the lives he affected by his love, kindness, and the most of all his personality.
