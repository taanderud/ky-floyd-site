Joan Burke Hall
Born: 12/09/1940
Died: 06/19/2006
Visitation: after 5:00 P.M. Tuesday at the Church
Funeral Service: Thursday, June 22, 2006 at 11:00 A.M. at the Rebecca Regular Baptist Church at Kite, Ky. by Old Regular Baptist Ministers
Interment: Wilburn Hall Cemetery at Kikte, Ky.
 
Joan Burke Hall, age 65, of Florahome, Fl., formerly of Bevinsville, Ky., wife of Carl David Hall, passed away Monday, June 19, 2006, at Hazard Appalachian Regional Medical Center, Hazard, Kentucky. She was born December 09, 1940, in Halo, Ky., the daughter of the late Garfield Burke and Marie Mitchell Burke. Mrs. Hall was a retired Food Store Manager in Keystone Heights Flordia. 

She is survived by her husband, Carl, a son, Rodney Hall, Columbia, Ky., a daughter, Carla Hoffman, Florahome, Fl., a brother, Ronnie Burke, Stark, Fl., two half-brothers, Roger Burke, Sunshine, Fl., Raymond Burke, Bevinsville, Ky., a sister, Joyce Allen, Jacksonville, Fl., six grandchildren, Amanda (Lucas) Bennett, Ashley Hall, Samantha Hall, Darienne Hall, Nicholas Price and Carrie Hughes, and three great grandchildren, Josie Bennett, Alek Bennett, and Isiah David Chaney. 

Funeral services for Joan Burke Hall will be conducted Thursday, June 22. 2006, at 11:00 a.m., at Rebecca Regular Baptist Church, Kite, Ky., with the Regular Baptist Ministers officiating. Burial will follow in the Wilburn Hall Cemetery, Kite, Ky., under the professional care of the Hall Funeral Home, Martin , Ky., caring for those arrangements.
