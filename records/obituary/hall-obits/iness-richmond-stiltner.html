Iness Richmond Stiltner
Born: 08/08/1917
Died: 04/19/2005
Visitation: after 6:00 P.M. Tuesday.
Funeral Service: Thursday, April 21, 2005 in the Hall Funeral Home with Johnny Collins and Bethel Bolen officiating.
Interment: Johnson Memorial Park at Pikeville, Ky.
 
Iness Richmond Stiltner, age 87, of Eastern, Ky, wife of Fleming K. Stiltner passed away Tuesday, April 19, 2005 at the Highlands Regional Medical Center, Prestonsburg, Kentucky. She was born August 08, 1917, in Portsmouth, Oh, the daughter of the late Bruce Richmond and Pearlie Rose Richmond Akers. Mrs. Stiltner was a homemaker and a member of the Lackey Freewill Baptist Church since 1973.

Survivors include, two sons, Herbert Lee Stiltner, Lakeland, Fl., Fleming Stiltner, Jr., Romulus, Mi., six daughters, Evelyn Stiltner Carty, Lima, Oh, Betty Jean Stiltner Hall, Hippo, Ky., Loretta Gaye Stiltner Meade, Rittman, Oh., Patricia Faye Stiltner, Eastern, Ky., Vivian Ruth Stiltner Draper, Stocking Bridge, Mi, Linda Stiltner Daniels, Eastern, Ky., two brothers, Bill Akers and Earl Akers both of Shepherdsville, Ky, two sisters, Pat Akers Greenwell, Tampa, Fl, Nora Akers Burnett, Shepherdsville, Ky, 17 grandchildren, 25 great grandchildren and 12 great-great grandchildren.

She was preceded in death by her husband, parents, two brothers, Fred Akers and Vance Richmond, and two sisters, Faye Richmond Hall and Eva Jean Akers Hall.

Funeral services for Iness Richmond Stiltner will be conducted Thursday, April 21, 2005, at 11:00 a.m., at the Hall Funeral Home Chapel, Martin, Ky, with the Clergymen Johnny Collins and Bethel Bolen officiating. Burial will follow in the Johnson Memorial Park, Pikeville, Ky, under the professional care of the Hall Funeral Home, Martin, Kentucky. Visitation is after 6:00 p.m., Tuesday here at The Funeral Home.
