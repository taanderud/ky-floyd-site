PHYLLIS ANN CENTERS
Born: 06/15/1936
Died: 11/02/2004
Visitation: from 2 - 10:00 P.M. Friday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: SATURDAY, NOVEMBER 6, 2004 AT 12:00 NOON IN THE CHAPEL WITH DON CRISP OFFICIATING.
Interment: Centers Family Cemetery at Hueysville, Ky.
 

Phyllis Anne Centers, age 68, of Martin, Ky, widow of Paul Channing Centers, passed away Tuesday, November 02, 2004, at The St. Elizabeth Medical Center, Hospice Unit, Covington, Kentucky. She was born June 15, 1936 in Craigsville, Va, the daughter of the late Lee Fields and Francis Reid Crisp.

Mrs. Centers was a former Bank Employee, Director of the Betsy Layne Senior Citizens and a member of the Faith Bible Church, Martin, Kentucky.

Survivors include one son, Paul David Centers, Somerset, Ky, two daughters, Donna Rose White, Florence, Ky, Barbara Anne Marks, Burlington, Ky, two sisters, Pat Shepherd, Martin, Ky, Janet Howard, Prestonsburg, Ky, and four grandchildren.

Floyd County Times

CENTERS, Phyllis Anne   Age 68, of Martin, widow of Paul Channing Centers, passed away, Tuesday, November 2, 2004, at the St. Elizabeth Medical Center, Hospice Unit, Covington, Kentucky. She was born June 15, 1936, in Craigsville, Virginia, the daughter of the late Lee Fields and Francis Reid Crisp. She was a former bank employee, a director of the Betsy Layne Senior Citizens, and a member of the Faith Bible Church, at Martin. Survivors include one son, Paul David Centers of Somerset; two daughters: Donna Rose (Jim) White of Florence, and Barbara Anne (Herbert) Marks of Burlington; two sisters: Pat Shepherd of Martin, and Janet Howard of Prestonsburg; and four grandchildren, Joshua Paul White, Jennifer Carroll Dawson, Christopher Paul Centers, and Briana Rose Marks. Funeral services for Phyllis Anne Centers will be conducted Saturday, November 6, at noon, at the Hall Funeral Home Chapel, at Martin, with Clergyman Don Crisp officiating. Burial will follow in the Centers Family Cemetery, at Hueysville, under the professional care of the Hall Funeral Home. Visitation is at the funeral home.  (FCT'S)
