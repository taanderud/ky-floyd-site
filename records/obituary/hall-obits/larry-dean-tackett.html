Larry Dean Tackett
Born: 05/12/1949
Died: 04/18/2005
Visitation: After 5:00 p.m. Saturday, April 23, 2005 here at Hall Funeral Home.
Funeral Service: 1:00 p.m. Sunday, April 24, 2005 here at Hall Funeral Home with Jerry Manns officiating.
Interment: Joe Reynolds Cemetery, Beaver, Ky.
 

Larry Douglas Tackett, age 55 of Beaver, Ky., the son of Catherine Reynolds Tackett of Beaver, Ky., and the late Paul Tackett, passed away Monday April 18, 2005 in the Carroll County Memorial Hospital, Carrollton, Ky. He was born May 12, 1949 in Prestonsburg, Ky. Mr. Tackett was a retired Coal Miner and US Army Veteran.

Survivors include one son, Garrick Tackett, Las Vegas, NV., two daughters, Tammy Mitchell Ruehrwein, Milford, OH, Kristie Nicole Tackett, Houston, TX, one brother, Winfred Tackett, Sturgis, Ky. one sister, Vivian Kay Newsome, Beaver, Ky., three grandchildren, Collin Ruehrwein, Clay Ruehrwein and Logan Jacob Tackett.

He was preceded in death beside his father one brother, Garry Dean Tackett and one sister, Rowena Tackett.
