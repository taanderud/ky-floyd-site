Hall Funeral Home Records

Emma Lou Clark
Born: 02/14/1937
Died: 03/12/2005
Visitation: after 5:00 P.M. Sunday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Tuesday, March 15, 2005 at 11:00 A.M. at the Hall Funeral Chapel with Tracy patton officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens at Ivel, Ky.
 

Emma Lou Clark age 68 of Betsy Layne, Ky. passed from this life on Saturday evening, march 12, 2005 at her residence. She was born February 14, 1937 at Honaker, Ky. the daughter of Cynthia Kidd Akers of Betsy Layne, Ky. and the late Jeff Akers.

She was retired from American Standard Corp. and was a member of the Betsy Layne Freewill Baptist Church.

She was preceded in death by her father, Jeff Akers and a brother, Ronald Akers.

Surviving: Husband: Adis Clark of Betsy Layne, Ky.  Son: Ronnie Clark of Prestonsburg, Ky. Daughters: Barb Lykens and Vickie Rose both of Betsy Layne, Ky. Mother: Cynthia Kidd Akers of Betsy Layne, Ky. Brother: Bud Akers Betsy Layne, Ky. Sisters: Hazel Cole of Harold, Ky. , Lil Coffman of Bowling Green, Ky. & Christine Kelly of Betsy Layne, Ky. Grandchildren: Brandi Lykens, Jayne Hale, Whitney Lykens, James Rose, Christi Lykens & Blake Rose Great Grandchild: Aubree Brown
