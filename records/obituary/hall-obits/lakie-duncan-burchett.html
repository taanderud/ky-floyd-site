LAKIE DUNCAN BURCHETT
Born: 03/21/1910
Died: 08/25/2004
Visitation: from 5 until 9:00 P.M. Friday at the Funeral Home and after 5:00 P.M. Saturday at the Church
Funeral Service: TOM'S CREEK FREEWILL BAPTIST CHURCH at Ivel, Ky. Sunday, August 29, 2004 at 1:00 P.M. with James Harmon and Chuck Ferguson officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens at Ivel, Kentucky
 
Lakie Duncan Burchett, age 94 of Ivel, Ky. entered eternal rest on Wednesday, August 25, 2004 at the Pikeville Medical Center. She was born on March 21, 1910 at Wonder, Ky. the daughter of the late John and Sally Trimble Duncan.

She had been a homemaker and was a member of the Freewill Baptist Church or 65 years, a member of the Tom's Creek Freewill Baptist Church since 1986.

She was preceded in death by, her husband: Guy Burchett in 1986.

Surviving are: A son, Ken Burchett of Lexington, Ky. and a daughter, Jean M. Blackburn of Endicott, Ky. 4 Grandchildren, 7 Great Grandchildren and 1 Great-Great Grandchild.

Funeral Services: Sunday, August 29, 2004 at 1:00 P.M. in the Tom's Creek Freewill Baptist CHurch at Ivel, Ky. with Re. James Harmon and Rev. Chuck Ferguson officiating. Burial in the Davidson Memorial Gardens at Ivel, Ky. Under the professional care of HALL FUNERAL HOME of Martin, Kentucky
