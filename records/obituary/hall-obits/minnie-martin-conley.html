Minnie Martin Conley
Died: 06/22/2004
Visitation: at the funeral home Thursday, 6/24/2004
Funeral Service: Friday, June 25, 2004 at 10:00 P.M. in the Funeral Home Chapel with Roy Harlow officiating.
Interment: Mimia Conley Cemetery at Hueysville, Ky.
 

Minnie Martin Conley, daughter of the late W.S. “Hoosh” Martin and Catherine “Sissy” Webb Martin, passed away on Tuesday, June 22, 2004 at the Bluegrass Hospice Care Center in Lexington, Ky. Minnie graduated from Maytown High School and Berea College. She taught home economics for two years and spent most of her life managing Conley’s Kentucky Food Store at Garrett, Kentucky.

She is survived by her husband of 61 years, Judge Hollie Conley of Eastern, Kentucky, One Son, Hollie Martin Conley of New York City, Three Daughters, Danise (Mark) White of Tazewell, Virgina, Judelle Conley of Lexington, Kentucky and Melanie (Ron) Warfield of Gibson City, Illinois. Also surviving are, Six Grandchildren: Holly (Bo) Renes, Chad White, Rebecca White, Scott Warfield, Robb (Sheila) Warfield and Leah Warfield. In addition there are six great grandchildren and many neices and nephews.

She was preceded in death by brothers, Henry, Oliver and Charlie Martin. Sisters, Golda Salyers, Lula Martin and Belva Martin.\
