Ted Thompson
Born: 05/12/1938
Died: 10/16/2004
Visitation: After 6:00 p.m. Monday, October 18, 2004 here at Hall Funeral Home.
Funeral Service: 11:00 a.m. Wednesday, October 20, 2004 here at Hall Funeral Home with Lonnie Badger officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens, Ivel, Ky.
 

Ted Thompson, age 66, of Satnviile, Ky., husband of Ruth Elizabeth Mayton Thompson, passed away Saturday, October 16, 2004 at his residence. He was born May 12, 1938 in Wheelwright, Ky., the son of the late George W. Thompson and Oma Hal Thompson. Mr. Thompson was a retired Road Construction Worrker for United Steel Workers Union, a U.S. Army Veteran, member of the Riverside Baptist Church, Tram, Ky., a member of the United Steel Workers Union.

In addition to his wife, Ruth Elizabeth, he is survived by one son, George R. Thompson, Stanville, Ky., one daughter, Rachel B. Thompson, Richmond, Ky., One brother, Tommy Thompson, Kite, Ky., One sister, Loretta Martin, Wayland, Ky.

In addition to his parents he is preceded in death by one brother, Fred Thompson.
