Hall Funeral Home Records

 

Phillip Marty Johnson
Born: 10/05/1976
Died: 09/11/2004
Visitation: After 10:00 a.m. Thursday, September 16, 2004 at the Wheelwright Freewill Baptist Church.
Funeral Service: 1:00 p.m. Friday, September 17, 2004 at the Wheelwright Freewill Baptist Church, with Louis Ferrari officiating.
Interment: Family Cemetery, Wheelwright, Ky.
 

PHILLIP MARTY JOHNSON, 27, OF NICHOLASVILLE, KY, FORMERLY WHEELWRIGHT, KY, PASSED AWAY SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 11, 2004, IN MELBOURNE, FLORDIA. HE WAS BORN OCTOBER 05, 1976 , IN MARTIN, KY, THE SON OF RICKY AND KATHY JOHNSON, WHEELWRIGHT, KENTUCKY.

IN ADDITION TO HIS PARENTS HE IS SURVIVED ONE BROTHER, JUSTIN JOHNSON, WHEELWRIGHT, KY, ONE SISTER, KACY JOHNSON, RICHMOND, KY, ONE NIECE, FRANKIE RAHKEL GOINES, WHEELWRIGHT, KY, BIOLOGICAL FATHER, MARTY GORE, RICHMOND, KY, TWO HALF-SISTERS, HANNAH GORE AND ABIGAIL GORE BOTH OF RICHMOND, KENTUCKY.
