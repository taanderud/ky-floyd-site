TOBY DEAN SLONE
Born: 07/22/1972
Died: 10/19/2004
Visitation: after 7:00 P.M. Thursday at the funeral home.
Funeral Service: Saturday, October 23, 2004 at 1:00 P.M. in the Funeral Home Chapel with Bobby Joe Spencer and Eugene Cook officiating.
Interment: Gethsemane Gardens at Prestonsburg, Ky.
 
Toby Dean Slone, age 32, of Prestonsburg, Ky, husband of Kelli Hammond Slone, passed away Tuesday, October 19, 2004, at the Highlands Regional Medical Center, Prestonsburg, Kentucky. He was born July 22, 1972 in Pikeville, Ky, the son of Charles Slone, Blue River, Ky, and Teresa Parsons Moss, Auxier, Kentucky. Mr. Slone was a construction worker for East Kentucky Excavation, Allen, Ky., a U.S. Army, Desert Storm Veteran, and a Member of the Middle Creek Baptist Church, Blue River, Kentucky.

In addition to his wife and parents he is survived by two daughters, Katelyn Graham Slone and Makinley Grace Slone both of Prestonsburg, Ky, his Step-father Michael Moss, Auxier, Ky, his Step-mother, Estalene Slone, Blue River, Ky, one brother, Jason ( Melisa) Slone, Auxier, Ky, his Step Brother, Damon Moss, Campbellville, Ky, one sister, Alicia Slone, Blue River, Ky, Grandparents, Barbara Slone, Prestonsburg, Ky,. Irvin Slone, Blue River, Ky, one niece, Mariah Hackworth and one nephew, Austin Slone both of Auxier, Ky, and many aunts and uncles.
