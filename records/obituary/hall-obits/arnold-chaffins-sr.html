ARNOLD CHAFFINS, SR.
Born: 05/06/1920
Died: 03/16/2005
Visitation: AFTER 5:00 P.M. THURSDAY AT THE FUNERAL HOME.
Funeral Service: SATURDAY, MARCH 19, 2005 AT 12:00 NOON IN THE HALL FUNERAL HOME CHAPEL WITH REGULAR BAPTIST MINISTERS OFFICIATING.
Interment: CHAFFINS CEMETERY AT ROCKFORK NEAR GARRETT, KY.
 

ARNOLD CHAFFINS, AGE 84 OF GARRETT, KY. PASSED FROM THIS LIFE ON WEDNESDAY, MARCH 16, 2005 ON THE CABELL-HUNTINGTON HOSPITAL IN HUNTINGTON, W.VA. AFTER A LONG ILLNESS. BORN MAY 6, 1920 AT GARRETT, KY. HE WAS THE SON OF THE LATE OLIVER AND ROXIE BOLEN CHAFFINS.

HE WAS A RETIRED FARMER AND OF THE BAPTIST FAITH.

SURVIVORS INCLUDE, ONE SON, ARNOLD JUNIOR CHAFFINS WICKER OF GARRETT, KY. ONE BROTHER, DEMPSEY CHAFFINS OF GARRETT, KY. THREE SISTERS, HAZEL REED OF GARRETT, KY., OPAL CHAFFINS OF WILLARD, OHIO AND BEATRICE MOORE OF MICHIGAN. ONE GRANDSON, PHILLIP CHAFFINS WICKER.

FUNERAL SERVICES: SATURDAY, MARCH 19, 2005 AT 12:00 NOON IN THE HALL FUNERAL HOME CHAPEL IN MARTIN, KY. WITH OLD REGULAR BAPTIST MINISTERS OFFICIATING. BURIAL IN THE CHAFFINS CEMETERY AT ROCKFORK NEAR GARRETT, KY. UNDER THE DIRECTION OF HALL FUNERAL HOME OF MARTIN, KY.
