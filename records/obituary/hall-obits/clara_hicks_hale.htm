CLARA HICKS HALE
Born: 10/03/1917
Died: 01/14/2006
Visitation: 2:00 P.M. until 4:30 P.M. and 6:30 P.M. until 9:00 P.M. Friday at the Funeral Home.
Funeral Service: Saturday, January 21, 2006 at 11:00 A.M. in the Hall Funeral Chapel with Chester Ray Varney officiating.
Interment: Hale Cemetery at Hueysville, Ky.
 

Clara Hicks Hale, age 88 of Hueysville, Ky. departed this life on Saturday, January 14, 2006 in Our Lady of the Way Hospital in Martin, Ky. after a long illness. She was born October 3, 1917 at Hippo, Ky. being the daughter of the late David "Dave" Hicks and Ruby Allen Hicks.

She was a homemaker and a member of the Hueysville Church of Christ.

Surviving family: Hern husband, Wornie Hale of Hueysville, Ky. One Son, Denzil Hale of Reynoldsburg, Ohio and One Daughter, Lillie McNutt of Hueysville, Ky. Brothers, Willis Hicks of Hippo, Ky. and Hargis Hicks of Ohio, Sister, Gladys Tussey of Utica, Ohio. 5 Grandchildren, 1 Great Grandchild.

Funeral Services: Saturday, January 21, 2006 at 11:00 A.M. in the Hall Funeral Home Chapel with Chester Ray Varney officiating. Burial in the Hale Cemetery at Hueysville, Ky. Under the professional care of Hall Funeral Home of Martin, Ky.

The family will receive visitors from 2:00 P.M. until 4:30 P.M. and 6:30 P.M. until 9:00 P.M. Friday at the Funeral Home.
