GOLDIE HALL
Born: 12/04/1912
Died: 12/10/2005
Visitation: after 5:00 P.M. Sunday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Tuesday, December 13, 2005 at 11:00 A.M. in the Zion Regular Baptist Church at Grethel, Ky. by Old Regular Baptist Ministers
Interment: Hall Cemetery at Grethel, Ky.
 

Goldie Hall, age 92 of Grethel, Ky. passed from this life on Saturday, December 12, 2005 in the McDowell Appalachian Hospital. She was born December 4, 1912 at Grethel, Ky. the daughter of the late Ben and Martha Hamilton Tackett.

She was a homemaker and a Member of the Zion Regular Baptist Church at Grethel, Ky.

She is survived by: One Son, Drexel Hall of Ivel, Ky. and One Daughter, Lois Gayle Newsome of Grethel, Ky. 20 Grandchildren, 30 Great Grandchildren, 9 Great Great Grandchildren, 2 Step Grandchildren & 7 Step Great Grandchildren.

She was preceded in death by: Husband, Curt Hall, Seven sons, Comus (Tony) Hall, Hershel Hall, Ronald Gene Hall, Carlos (Cotton) Hall, Don Edward (Donnie) Hall, Carmel Hall & Winford Hall. Daughter, Barbara Hall.

Funeral Services: Tuesday, December 13, 2005 at 11:00 A.M. in the Zion Old Regular Baptist Church at Grethel, Ky. with Ministers of the Church officiating. Burial in the Hall Cemetery at Grethel, Ky. Under the professional care of Hall Funeral Home of Martin, Ky.

Visitation at the funeral home after 5:00 P.M. Sunday with services nightly at 7:00 P.M.
