SHERMAN DALE GUNNELS
Born: 12/31/1961
Died: 06/07/2006
Visitation: after 6:00 P.M. Friday, June 9, 2006 in the the Funeral Home Chapel.
Funeral Service: Saturday, June 10, 2006 at 1:00 P.M. at the Rogers Funeral Home in Belfry, Ky. with Freddie Lewis officiating.
Interment: Harris Family Cemetery at Ransom, Ky.
 
Sherman Dale Gunnels age 44 of Ransom, Ky. formerly of Langley, Ky. passed from this life on Wednesday, June 7, 2006 at Pikeville, Ky. He was born December 31, 1961 at Langley, Ky. the son of the late Bill Taylor and Ruth (Ousley) Gunnels.

He was a construction contractor for Wright Concrete of Pikeville and was of the Freewill Baptist Faith.

Survivors include his wife, Betty Harris Plymal Gunnels of Ransom, Ky. Three brothers, Charles (Linda) Gunnels of Salyersville, Ky., Ivan (Debby) Gunnels of Eastern, Ky. and Jessie (Judy) Gunnels of Indiana. Three sisters, Maggie Stevens of Texas, Lillie Blizzard of Martin, Ky. and Ellen Mae (Danny) Slone of Langley, Ky. Also surviving are a host of nieces and nephews.

Funeral Services: Saturday, June 10, 2006 at 1:00 P.M. in the Rogers Funeral Home at Belfry, Ky. with Freddie Lewis officiating. Burial in the Harris Family Cemetery at Ransom, Ky.

Family and friends will serve as pallbearers.
