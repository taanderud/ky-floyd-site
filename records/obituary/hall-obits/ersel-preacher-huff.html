Elder Ersel Huff

ELDER ERSEL HUFF, AGE 81, OF DRIFT, KY, HUSBAND OF EDITH KIDD HUFF, PASSED AWAY THURSDAY, MAY 13, 2004, AT THE CENTRAL BAPTIST HOSPITAL, LEXINGTON, KENTUCKY. HE WAS BORN APRIL 25, 1923 IN HALO, KY, THE SON OF THE LATE JOHN C. HUFF AND MARY JOHNSON HUFF. MR. HUFF WAS A RETIRED COAL MINER, AN OLD REGULAR BAPTIST MINISTERS, A MEMBER OF THE OLD BEAVER REGULAR BAPTIST CHURCH, A MEMBER OF THE D.A.V. CHAPTER 128 AT GARRETT, KENTUCKY AND AN WORLD WAR II ARMY VETERAN.

SURVIVORS INCLUDE HIS WIFE, EDITH KIDD HUFF, SIX SONS, DENNIS (ANNA JO) HUFF, DAVID HUFF, AND ALEX HUFF ALL OF DRIFT, KY, EZEKIEL (SANDRA) HUFF, JOHN (CATHY) HUFF BOTH OF HOMER, MI, NOAH (CHERI) HUFF, HILLSDALE, MI, SIX DAUGHTERS, DORIS (WILLIS) MILLER AND PHYLLIS ROBINSON BOTH OF HOMER, MI, ROXIE (EDDIE) CONN AND MARTHA ROBINSON BOTH OF DRIFT, KY, MARY (JASEL) DYE, GRETHEL, KY, BESSIE (RODNEY) HOWELL OF LIGON, KY. TWO SISTERS, DOROTHY HAMILTON, DRIFT, KY, GLORIA COOK, BARSTOW, CA, 41 GRANDCHILDREN AND 28 GREAT GRANDCHILDREN.

IN ADDITION TO HIS PARENTS HE WAS PRECEDED IN DEATH BY THREE BROTHER, HENRY P. HUFF, PERCY HUFF, WILLIAM H. HUFF, TWO SON-IN-LAWS, HARLEY ROBINSON AND BALLARD JAMES ROBINSON, AND ONE GRANDCHILD, RODNEY HOWELL,
