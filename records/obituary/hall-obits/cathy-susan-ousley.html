CATHY SUSAN OUSLEY
Born: 06/12/1956
Died: 05/03/2006
Visitation: after 5:00 P.M. Thursday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Staurday, May 6, 2006 at 1:00 P.M. in the Hall Funeral Home with Bill Campbell and Russ Taylor
Interment: Ousley Family Cemetery at Martin, Ky.
 
Cathy Susan Ousley, age 49, of Martin, Ky., wife of Dormal Ousley, passed away Wednesday, May 03, 2006 at her residence. She was born June 12, 1956 in McDowell, Ky., the daughter of Katherine Wright Newman, Martin, Ky., and the late Fred O'Bryan. Mrs. Ousley was a Hairdresser, and she owned and operated Cathy's Cutting Edge.

Survivors include her husband, mother, a son, Christopher Michael Ousley, Martin, Ky., a daughter, Ashley Ousley, Winchester, Ky., a brother, Bobby O'Bryan, Indianapolis, In., a sister, Brenda McKenzie, Louisa, Ky., a half-sister, Debra Newman, Lexington, Ky., and a grandson, Jonathan Michael Ousley.

Her father and a brother, James Edward O'Bryan, preceded her in death.

Funeral services for Cathy Susan Ousley will be conducted Saturday, May 06, 2006, at 1:00 p.m., at the Hall Funeral Home Chapel, Martin, Ky., with the Clergyman

Bill Campbell. Burial will follow in the Ousley Family Cemetery, Martin, Ky., under the professional care of the Hall Funeral Home, Martin, Kentucky. Visitation is after 5:00 p.m., Thursday here at the Funeral Home.
