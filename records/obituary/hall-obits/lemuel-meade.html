Hall Funeral Home Records

Lemuel Meade
Born: 10/13/1915
Died: 09/26/2004
Visitation: After 6:00 p.m. Monday, September 27, 2004 here at Hall Funeral Home with night service 7:00 p.m. Tuesday
Funeral Service: 11:00 a.m. Wednesday, September 29, 2004 in the Betsy Layne Church of Christ, Betsy Layne, Ky., with Tommy Spears and Linro Meade officiating.
Interment: Hamilton Family Cemetery, Honaker, Ky.

   

LEMUEL MEADE, AGE 88, OF HAROLD, KY, HUSBAND OF THE LATE DOLLY MAE HAMILTON MEADE, PASSED AWAY SUNDAY, SEPTEMBER 26, 2004, AT THE HIGHLANDS REGIONAL MEDICAL CENTER, PRESTONSBURG, KENTUCKY. HE WAS BORN OCTOBER 13, 1915, IN HONAKER, KY, THE SON OF THE LATE ROBERT MEADE AND MYRTIE CASE MEADE. MR. MEADE WAS A RETIRED CARPENTER AND A MEMBER OF THE BETSY LAYNE CHURCH OF CHRIST.

SURVIVORS INCLUDE FOUR SONS, ALBON MEADE, STANVILLE, KY, CLOYDE MEADE, BETSY LAYNE, KY, DALE L. MEADE, KODAK, TN, RICK MEADE, HAROLD, KY, THREE BROTHERS, RANSOM MEADE, BETSY LAYNE, KY, WOODROW MEADE, PERRYVILLE, OH, LINRO MEADE, CLARKSVILLE, OH, SIX SISTERS, ESTELLE SHENCK, PERRYVILLE, OH, ROSIE JANE JACKSON, NEW LONDON, OH, SILTANIA YATES, PERRYVILLE, OH, ALMAS SHEPHERD, GLENMONT, OH, ETHEL HAMILTON, DANA, KY, MURIEL PERKINS, BETSY LAYNE, KY, 12 GRANDCHILDREN AND 21 GREAT GRANDCHILDREN.
