<>MARTHA WILLIAMSON

MARTHA WILLIAMSON
Born: 08/09/1921
Died: 10/16/2004
Visitation: AFTER 5:00 P.M. Sunday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Tuesday, October 19, 2004 at 11:00 A.M. in the Funeral Home Chapel with Rev. Ted Shannon officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens at Ivel, Ky.

   

Martha Williamson age 83 of Prestonsburg, Ky. passed away on Saturday at the Highlands Regional Medical Center in Prestonsburg, Ky. She was born in Martin COunty on August 9, 1921 the daughter of the late Jeff and Tishie Boyd Williamson.

Survivors are: One Son, Michael Rodriguez of Betsy Layne, Ky. and One Sister, Geneva Ridener of Estill, Ky. a Grandson, Timothy Michael Rodriguez.
