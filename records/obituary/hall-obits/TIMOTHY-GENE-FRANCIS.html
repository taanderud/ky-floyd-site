TIMOTHY GENE FRANCIS
Born: 09/05/1956
Died: 05/22/2006
Visitation: after 6:00 P.M. Wednesday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Friday, May 26, 2006 at 1:00 P.M. in the Funeral Home Chapel with Randy Osborne and Larry Adams officiating.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens at Ivel, Ky
 
TIMOTHY GENE FRANCIS, AGE 50, OF GARRETT, SON OF WINCHESTER AND LUCILLE CAMPBELL FRANCIS, DIED MONAY, MAY 22 IN THE KINGS DAUGHTER MEDICAL CENTER, ASHLAND. HE WAS BORN SEPTEMBER 5, 1955 IN MARTIN. TIMOTHY WAS CURRENTLY EMPLOYED AS A STATE AUDITOR FOR THE KENTUCKY DEPT. OF REVENUE.

IN ADDITION TO HIS PARENTS HE SURVIVED BY ONE BROTHER GARY DEAN FRANCIS, GARRETT, THREE NIECES, DEANNA JILL FRANCIS, LUCIANNA GAYLE RODDY, AND TIFFANY DANIELA DUNHAM, ONE NEPHEW, GARY NICHOLAS FRANCIS, FOUR GREAT NEPHEWS, JACOB LOGAN RODDY, CHANCE WESLEY RODDY, JEFFORY (TREY) ALDRICH III, DREW ALTON DUNHAM, AND ONE GREAT NIECE, DANIELLE BROOKE DUNHAM.

FUNERAL SERVICE FOR TIMOTHY GENE FRANCIS WILL BE CONDUCTED FRIDAY, MAY 26 AT 1 P.M. IN THE HALL FUNERAL HOME CHAPEL, MARTIN, WITH RANDY OSBORNE AND LARRY ADAMS OFFICITATING. BURIAL WILL FOLLOW IN THE DAVIDSON MEMORIAL GARDENS, IVEL, WITH THE HALL FUNERAL HOME CARING FOR THOSE ARRANGEMENTS.
