LOVELLA WEBB HANSFORD
Born: 02/14/1926
Died: 07/29/2004
Visitation: after 6:00 P.M. Friday, 7/20/04 in the chapel.
Funeral Service: SUNDAY, AUGUST 1, 2004 AT 1:00 P.M. IN THE FUNERAL HOME CHAPEL, WITH LOUIE FERRARI, JOHN ALLEN, RED MORRIS AND OTHERS OFFICIATING.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens at Ivel, Kentucky
 

LOVELLA WEBB HANSFORD, AGE 78, OF WAYLAND, KY, WIFE OF GEORGE R. HANSFORD, PASSED AWAY THURSDAY, JULY 29, 2004, AT THE MCDOWELL APPALACHIAN REGIONAL HOSPITAL, MCDOWELL, KENTUCKY. SHE WAS BORN FEBRUARY 14, 1926, IN KNOTT COUNTY, KY, THE DAUGHTER OF THE LATE STEVE WEBB AND STELLA KISER WEBB. MRS. HANSFORD WAS A HOMEMAKER AND A HAD BEEN A MEMBER OF THE MARTIN BRANCH FREEWILL BAPTIST CHURCH, ESTILL, KY, SINCE 1970.

SURVIVORS INCLUDE HER HUSBAND, GEORGE R. HANSFORD, TWO DAUGHTERS, SHARON FIELDS, LEXINGTON, KY, KAREN (DARRELL) KESTER, WAYLAND, KY, TWO BROTHERS, LARRY WEBB AND PAUL WEBB BOTH OF WEST VIRGINIA, SIX SISTERS, JEANETTE BEVERLY, SPRINGFIELD, OH, LINDA THOMPKINS, LAVINIA MAYSE, AND VIVIAN COOK ALL OF NORTH RIDEGFIELD, OH, JOAN LEFLER, CLEVELAND, OH, JANE COOK, NORTH CAROLINA, FOUR GRANDCHILDREN, CURTIS G. PACK, TANYA KESTER, CHRIS PACK, NICOLE RISTER, AND FOUR GREAT GRANCHILDREN, AMBER MEADE, ZACHARY CASTLE, KATELYN PACK, AND BRENNEN PERKINS.

SHE WAS PRECEDED IN DEATH BY HER PARENTS AND ONE SISTER, GEORGIA CONLEY.
