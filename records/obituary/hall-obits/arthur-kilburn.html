ARTHUR KILBURN
Born: 08/20/1933
Died: 03/07/2005
Visitation: after 6:00 P.M. Monday at the Funeral Home.
Funeral Service: Wedneday, March 10, 2005 at 1:00 P.M. in the Funeral Chapel by Ellis Stevens and Levi Meade.
Interment: Davidson Memorial Gardens at Ivel, Kentucky
 

Arthur Kilburn age 71 of Arkasas Creek, Martin, Ky. passed from this life on Monday Morning, March 8, 2005 in the Cabell - Huntington Hospital in Huntington, W.Va. after a long illnes. He was born August 8, 1933 at Martin, Kentucky being the son of the late Hardy and Cosetta Pitts Kilburn.

He is a retired coal miner.

Surviving are: The wife, Inez Yates Kilburn

Sons: Arthur, Jr., Paul Douglas, Marion & Hardy Kiblburn Daughter: Martha Etta Carroll, 12 Grandchildren, 7 great Grandchildren
