    Mrs. Kathryn Osborne Hall

Born: 12/01/1924
Died: 07/08/2004
Visitation: after 3:00 P.M. Saturday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Sunday, July 11, 2004 at the Funeral Chapel with Roy Harlow officiating.
Interment: Entombment in Highlands Memorial Park at Staffordsville, Ky.

KATHRYN OSBORNE HALL, AGE 79, OF MARTIN, KY, WIDOW OF JOHN C. HALL, PASSED AWAY THURSDAY, JULY 8, 2004, AT HER RESIDENCE. SHE WAS BORN DECEMBER 01, 1924 IN HUNTINGTON, WV, THE DAUGHTER OF THE LATE BERT OSBORNE AND IRENE CHALMERS OSBORNE. SHE WAS A FORMER BOOKEEPER AND CO-OWNER OF HALL FUNERAL HOME, MARTIN, KENTUCKY.

SURVIVORS INCLUDE TWO SONS, JOHN C. HALL, JR, AND HIS WIFE GLORIA, TOMMY HALL AND HIS WIFE BURNETTA , MARTIN, KY, ONE DAUGHTER, KIMBERLY ANN HALL, SALT LAKE CITY, UT, ONE SISTER, LOIS DRUMM, CINCINNATI, OH, TWO GRANDCHILDREN, KARA KATHRYN HALL AND COURTNEY BLAIR HALL.

SHE WAS PRECEDED IN DEATH BY HER HUSBAND, PARENTS, ONE BROTHER, B.R. OSBORNE, ONE SISTER, PATSY OSBORNE AND ONE NEPHEW ROBERT KEITH OSBORNE.
