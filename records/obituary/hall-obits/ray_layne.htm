RAY LAYNE
Born: 06/09/1924
Died: 03/21/2005
Visitation: after 6:00 P.M. Wednesday at the Funeral Home, MASONIC SERVICES AT 6:00 P.M. THURSDAY.
Funeral Service: Friday, March 25, 2005 at 11:00 A.M. in the Funeral Home Chapel with Regular Baptist Ministers.
Interment: Turner Cemetery at Dema, Ky.
 
Ray Layne, age 80, of Dema, KY, husband of Mary Slone Layne, passed away Monday, March 21, 2005, at the McDowell Appalachian Regional Hospital, McDowell, Kentucky. He was born June 09, 1924 in Dema, KY, the son of the late Ben Layne and Elizabeth Terry Layne. Mr. Layne was a Retired Coal Miner, a member of the UMWA Local # 1741, the James W. Alley Masonic Lodge, # 869, Wayland, KY, and Scottish Rite Freemasonary of Lexington, KY, and a Kentucky Colonel.
