NORALENE TACKETT HAMILTON
Born: 09/29/1932
Died: 01/03/2006
Visitation: after 5:00 P.M. Wedneday at the Funeral Home
Funeral Service: Friday, January 6, 2006 at 11:00 A.M. In the Zion Regular Baptist Church at Grethel, Ky. with Regular Baptist Ministers
Interment: Martin Cemetery at Martin, Ky.
 

Noralene Tackett Hamilton, age 73 of Prestonsburg, Ky. passed away on Tuesday, January 3, 2006 in the U.K. Medical Center in Lexington, Ky. She was born September 29, 1932 at Beaver, Ky. the daughter of the late William "Bill" Tackett and Hattie Hamilton Tackett.

She was a homemaker and a member of the Zion Old Regular Baptist Church at Grethel, Ky.

She is survived by: Three Daughters, Wanda Sexton, Debbie Johnson and Janet Salisbury all of Prestonsburg, Ky. Five Brothers, Donald Ray tackett of Grethel, Ky., Bobby Tackett of ALabama, John D. Tackett of Hi Hat, Ky., Ronnie Tackett of Wheelwright, Ky., Randy Tackett of Hi Hat, Ky. and Larry Tackett of Teaberry, Ky. Three Sisters, Irene Bohn of Lahabra, Ca., Patty Fowler of Indianapolis, Indiana and Billie Faye Henson of Prestonsburg, Ky.Grandchildren: Leanna Newsome, Ginger Scott, Jason Johnson, Rachel Salisbury & Sarah Salisbury, Great Grandchildren: Patrick Johnson, Derek Newsome, Megan Scott & Alex Scott

Funeral Services: Friday, January 6, 2005 at 11:00 A.M. inn the Zion Regular Baptist CHurch at Grethel,Ky.. with Regular Baptist Ministers officiating. Burial in the Martin Cemetery at Martin, Ky. Under the professional care of Hall Funeral Home of Martin, Ky.

Visitation will be at the Funeral Home after 5:00 P.M. Wednesday. An prior to services Friday at the Church.
