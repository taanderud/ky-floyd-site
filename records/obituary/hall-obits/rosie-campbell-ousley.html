            Rosie Campbell Ousley
Born: 03/06/1912
Died: 12/07/2005
Visitation: after 4:00 P.M. Friday at the funeral home and prior to services on Sunday at the church.
Funeral Service: Sunday, December 11, 2005 at 11:00 A.M. in the Cold Springs Regular Baptist Church at Martin, Ky. with Regular Baptist Ministers officiating
Interment: Oscar Ousely Cemetery
 
Rosie Campbell Ousley, age 93, of Martin, Ky., widow of Oscar Ousley, passed away Wednesday, December 07, 2005, at the Highlands Regional Medical Center, Prestonsburg, Kentucky. She was born March 06, 1912 in Hippo, Ky., the daughter of the late James Campbell and Jennie Prater Campbell. Mrs. Ousley was a homemaker and member of the Cold Springs Regular Baptist Church, Martin, Kentucky.

Survivors include four sons, Miley Ousley, Claypool In., Beecher Ousley, Delano Ousley, and Dormal Ousley all of Martin, Ky., three daughters, Mavis Spriggs Hicks, Eastern, Ky., Alma Jean Kost, Cleveland, Oh., Bonnie Condra, Winchester, Ky., one sister, Rebecca Templin, Newport, Tn., 20 grandchildren, several great grandchildren and great-great grandchildren.

Preceding her in death were her husband, parents, one son, Corneluis Ousley, four brothers, Hargis Campbell, Herald Campbell, Earl Campbell and Curtis Campbell.

Funeral services for Rosie Campbell Ousley will be conducted Sunday, December 11, 2005, at 11:00 a.m., at the Cold Springs Regular Baptist Church, Martin, Ky., with the Regular Baptist Ministers officiating. Burial will follow in the Oscar Ousley Cemetery, Martin, Ky., under the professional care of the Hall Funeral Home. Visitation is after 4:00 p.m., Friday here at the Funeral Home.
