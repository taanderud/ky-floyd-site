1800

1807 - Clay County established from Floyd, Knox, and Madison.

1808 - Courthouse burned and records were lost.

After the courthouse burned, court was held in private residences until the new courthouse could be established. The Annals of Floyd County 1800-1826 contains the early court records in an easy to read form.
