A woman & her baby are buried here; toward the bottom of the hill, set apart from the other graves. Legend tells us that her husband didn't have a grave in which to bury his wife and child, and that the Meade family offered him a plot in their cemetery. There doesn't seem to be any marking. My Mom says that growing up, there was never a marker.
