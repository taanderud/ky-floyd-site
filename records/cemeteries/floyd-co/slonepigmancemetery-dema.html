Slone Pigman Cemetery

Burial Plots

Dema, Floyd County, Kentucky GPS Location: N 37 25.123 W 082 47.786

Submitted by Keith Short

The Slone Pigman Cemetery is located on a hill next to a dwelling at 5302 KY Route 7. The cemetery is in Floyd County and it is near the intersection of KY 7 & KY 899 near the Floyd-Knott County line.

There is a small gravel lot across the highway from 5302 KY Route 7 to park your car to visit the cemetery. Do not drive your vehicle on the one lane gravel road. This is a private road for those who live on Lick Skillet Hollow.

Go across the highway from the gravel lot and walk on the one lane gravel road until you see abandoned water well on your right. On your left, you will see a path that goes uphill. Walk on that uphill path and it will take you to the front entrance of the cemetery.
