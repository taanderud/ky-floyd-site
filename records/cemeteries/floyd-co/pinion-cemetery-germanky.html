Pinion Cemetery

German, KY

This is my husband Great Grandfather. His property in German was bought by the Corp for the backwater of Dewey Lake. His son, Hentz Pinion would not let them relocate this cemetery, and now it is an Island. Can only be reached by boat and is in really bad shape due to this fact

Submitted by Mary Pinion
