Preston Family Cemetery Johnson County, KY

Just off Rt. 40 at the end of Preston Street are several large graveyards. One of these is behind a locked gate, another on the hillside. One section is full of very old stones that are for the most part unreadable. One that I could read was that of

NAME BIRTH DEATH NOTES
PRESTON, Moses  05-02-1797  11-23-1868  
GREEN, Dr. ???  03-05-1911  12-05-1934  
GREEN, Beulah  12-23-1903  11-14-1912
