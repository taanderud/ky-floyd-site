SPEARS CEMETERY

Williams Branch, Abbott Creek, Prestonsburg, Ky.

Directions: Take the Left fork of Abbott to Williams Branch, turn left. Take Williams Branch until the road turns into the creek. Walk up the creek until you reach the 3rd barn. To your left, the Spears Cemetery sets hidden in the tall brush and trees on a small flat area. It's nearly impossible to see during the summer months.
