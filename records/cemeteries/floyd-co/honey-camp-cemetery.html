NOTE* I was told by one of my Mullins' relatives that this piece of property was purchased by Albert and Nanie just for burial reasons. It is very hard to find. A local kid who lived in this hollow in Honeycamp showed it to me. As you go up Honeycamp it's the first road (a dirt road) that branches out to the right. About 100 yards or so up the hill, you'll find a metal gate, there's a little metal bridge about 3 ft. in length that crosses a little creek. Cemetery is located through a little pathway onward toward a hillside. It is grown up with bushes, trees, etc.
