Coburn-Inmon Cemetery

"INMON CEMETERY" also known as the "Coburn Cemetery"

This cemetery is located on a hill in Rock Fork, Knotts County, Ky. The location is off Rt.80 at the Rock Fork Freewill Baptist Church, turn into Church off Rt. 80, turn right at Church, go down the road till you come to the first lane that turns left up the hill, and it is about one-quarter mile up the hill. If you are old timer and know some of the history of the area this Cemetery is in then you may know it as the "COBURN CEMETERY" as that is what I was informed of in Dec. 1998..(very rough lane to top of hill) by Darrell Music
