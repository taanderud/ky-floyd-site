AUXIER CEMETERY # 8 Floyd Co, Located on old U.S. 23, on Floyd Johnson Co Line on Johns Creek, south side, there is a high metal fence and locked gate, the cemetery is on a knoll approximately one mile up Johns Creek, across from the home of Harold Auxier. submitted by Bobby W. Wells
