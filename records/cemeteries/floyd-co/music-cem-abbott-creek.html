OBIT: THEODORE MUSIC Services were conducted at 11:00 a.m. Tuesday, June 26, 1973 in the Enterprise Baptist Church on Fourth Street for Theodore Music who passed away Saturday, June 23, at his home at Staffordsville after an extended illness. The son of the late James and Ellen Baldridge Music, he was born June 23 1890 and was a member of the Enterprise Baptist Church. Surviving are his wife, Nellie Pennington Music, four daughters, Mrs. Edna Skrunks of Michigan; Mrs. Dorothy Fultz, of Germantown, Ky; Mrs. Barbara Shepherd of Packerton, Indiana; Miss Audrey music of East Point, and 12 grandchildren. Burial was in the Music Cemetery at East Point, with Reverends Willis Tackett and Sammy Baker officiating at the services. Arrangements were under the direction of the Jones-Preston Funeral Home.
