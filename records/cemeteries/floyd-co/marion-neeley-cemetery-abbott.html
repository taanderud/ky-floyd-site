Marion Neeley Cemetery

Left Hand fork of Abbott

Directions: take the left hand fork of Abbott until you reach Williams Branch.

The trail to the cemetery is right where you turn onto Williams Branch. The trail is

on the right side of the road, straight up the hill. Follow the trail up and it turns to the left, right up to the cemetery. It's a short walk, but steep. Cemetery is not visible from the road.

Photo's available upon request.
