Fitzpatrick Cemetery - Middle Creek

 

Directions: On Middle Creek (Mtn. Parkway)

                 Going West from Prestonsburg towards Salyersville. Go past the Middle Creek                  battlefield area and soon you will see a hydrofracking plant on the right side of the road.                  Pull into the plant and follow the paved road behind the plant. There is a trail to the                  cemetery on the left. You can either 4-wheel drive up or walk.

                 There is actually 4 dfferent family cemeteries up there. A Shepherd, Poe, Marsillett and                  Fitzpatrick. They are all together in the same area, with the Marsillett and Fitzpatrick with                  fences' around them. There is also some old graves scattered which are very hard to read.                  One of the surnames is Sparks...
