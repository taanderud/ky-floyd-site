Birth Record of Anthony Hall

Submitted by Bob Hall

As you look at this document see the entry beside March 11. This record was obtained for the Mormon Church in Salt Lake City Utah via microfilm.

The same roll of micro-film has Anthony Hall marrying Mary Hepple on Nov 13, 1744. It lists the birth of two other children to Anthony and Mary. Randall born June 9, 1748 and Thomas born May 5, 1750. All entries list Kirkhill Parish of Scotland as place of residence.
