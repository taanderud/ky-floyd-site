Which Conflict?

Your ancestor's birth year guides you which war he may have fought in. These are general guidelines as some men and women who served were younger or older and may fall outside these ranges.

  • Born 1726-1767: Revolutionary War
  • Born 1762-1799: War of 1812
  • Born 1796-1831: Mexican War
  • Born 1811-1848: Civil War
  • Born 1848-1881: Spanish-American War
  • Born 1849-1885: Philippine Insurrection
  • Born 1872-1900: WWI
  • Born 1872-1900: WWI
  • Born 1877-1925: WWII
  • Born 1900-1936: Korean War
  • Born 1914-1955: Vietnam War

Civil War Monument

This Ivy Mountain Battlefield monument is located on US Route 23 between Prestonsburg and Pikeville near the Ivel Post Office and the mouth of Ivy Creek. It was here, on November 8th, 1861, that the Battle of Ivy Mountain was fought.
