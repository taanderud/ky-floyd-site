Prestonsburg "Blackcats" Football Team - 1948, Being shared by Floyd "Mike" Horn. He was a member of the Prestonburg Blackcats. He was born and raised in Floyd Co. He loves to reminisce about his childhood and growing up in West Prestonsburg. He says his fondest memories whenhe was as a young man was hanging out at the depot that was located in West Prestonsburg.
