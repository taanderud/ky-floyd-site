Allen Grade School - 1948

Submitted by Clyde F Hancock

Eighth grade class of the old Allen Grade School taken in the spring of 1948. Mr. Otis "Ott" Spurlock was the Principle and Mrs. Opal May was the home room teacher. Clyde Hancock Top row second from left

Extra Information

In the picture submitted by Clyde Hancock I know a few:

Top row to the right of Clyde is Mickey Childers of Emma, 6th over is a Baldridge subject and  8th is Tommy Westfall of Allen and 9th is Curt Reed Burchett of Emma.

2nd row 1st of left is a Bottie Cartmell who lived near current Allen Elementary by rivers edge. First row and 1st on left is a Scottie Hamilton of Emma.

Info submitted by Ronald Mosley
