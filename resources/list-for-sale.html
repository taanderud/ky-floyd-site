Book: Never Forget to Remember

Never Forget to Remember (A genealogy of the Fleming Families of SW VA, Eastern KY and Southern WV - 2nd edition) is available for purchase. The book includes data for the 7 known children of Robert & Elizabeth (Stambaugh) Fleming & a host of their descendants. Surnames also included are Mullins, Rose, Stanley, Vanover, Bentley, Hall, Johnson, and many others. It is 8-1/2 x 11, soft cover, contains 440 pages (including index), numerous obituaries and photos. To order send a check or money order to Janice F. Lambert, 21193 Vances Mills Rd, Abingdon, VA 24211. Cost is $48.50 per book (postpaid). This book is a record of our heritage and can be handed down to future generations.

Further information may be obtained by e-mailing me at pastseeker@comcast.net.

Silver Bear Books

Presents author Terri Robinson's genealogy and family history books about her roots and heritage in eastern Kentucky. First published in 2002, Kentucky Tapestry, Threads of My Ancestor's Pathway was reissued in 2010 as a second edition with updated facts, photos and information about Miller, McVeigh, Hall, Wallen, Hale, Caudill and related family names in and around Floyd County.

Field Gap, Pathway to Her Spirit's Home, Haunted Hunting Ground. and Fire Keeper's Basket are in very limited supply through www.silverbearbooks.com.

A second website is a collection of family photos, portrait's and obituary clippings for these surnames and can be seen on www.silverbearbooksgallery.com

Dancing In Spider's Web was released in late August, 2012 and is available through www.silverbearbooks.com and www.amazon.com.

For a discount, please contact Terri Robinson at info@silverbearbooks.com
