The home of William Salisbury (1783-1860) at Hunter, Kentucky on Left Beaver Creek. Sitting, from left, are Green Salisbury, son of William Salisbury and Rhoda Sizemore Salisbury; Mrs. Morgan Salisbury; her children Nick (on lap), Morgan Jr., Betty, and Robert Salisbury. Next to them are Oscar and Sandy Martin. Back row, from left: Jim Salisbury and son Herbert, Bud Salisbury, Mrs. Monroe Stumbo, Polly Salisbury Martin, Jane Salisbury Spurlock, Viola Stevens Hamilton, James Salisbury, Hiram Salisbury, and Morgan Martin. On the left mounted on the horse is Boone Webb.
