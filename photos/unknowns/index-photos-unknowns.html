The last young man on the right by the gentleman in the suit, was my father Harry E  Hager, he was the son of Frank & Lula Hale Hager, grand son of James M and Armintha Musick/Music Hager his paternal grandparents.  His maternal grandparents were John Emery and Elizabeth Alley Hale. Dad's parents Frank and Lula died young and Dad and his sister's-Minnie and Ethel and his brother Jimmy were sent to the Masonic Orphanage near Louisville. I don't know who the others are.  Contributed By: Barbara Hager Estep
