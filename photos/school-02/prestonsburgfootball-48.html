Prestonsburg Football Team - 1948

Submitted by the Neal's

Prestonsburg "Blackcats" Football Team - 1948, Being shared by Floyd "Mike" Horn. He was a member of the P'burg Blackcats. He was born and raised in Floyd Co. He loves to reminisce about his childhood and growing up in W. Prestonsburg. He says his fondest memories when he was a young man was hanging out at the depot that was located in W. Prestonsburg.
