Stone Coal School Class of 1934_35
Delvon H. Jones, 29765 Creekwood Road, Madison, AL 35757, shares this 1934 photo of the Stone Coal School at Garrett, Kentucky. First row, l-r: Shannon Hicks, Lloyd Hicks, and Raymond Pratt. Second row, l-r: Bo Scott, Jim Wicker, Ruth Pratt, Rhodie Pratt, Pauline Fraizer, Cornie Napier, Emma Smith, Edna Ratliff, Rheta Hicks, Lessie Hicks, Gladys Wicker, Cindy Whitt, and Ruth Hoover. Third row, l-r: Ray Moore, Olive Bently, ? Fraizer, Mable Conley, Artie Hoover, unknown, Herbert Hoover, Nora Hoover, and Mae Hoover. The teacher was Howard Allen.
