Faye Caudill Combs

Picture taken in Florida on vacation about 1940, wife of Irvin Combs, daughter of Evelyn Cox & Robert Lee Caudill, Granddaughter of Florence Martin & John Cox and Minda Brown & Willard Caudill. Faye was born at Fed, Ky. on April 23, 1920, died June, 1989 in Fla.
