Floyd County Family Pictures

John Riley Combs, born Oct. 09,1877, in Litt Carr, Knott Co., Ky. died April 06, 1947. Husband of Mary Turner, their home was in Orkney, Ky. He was son of Andrew Combs & Nancy Francis, he was one of 13 sons. His children are Dorothy Moore, Herbert, Irvin & Evelyn Griffie.
