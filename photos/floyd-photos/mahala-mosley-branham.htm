Mahala Mosley Branham, born 22 Nov 1822, Tennessee, and died 12 Dec 1912, Dickenson Co., Virginia, parents names unverified. She was marr. to John C. Branham, 1818-1908, a son of William or James William Branham and Charity Gibson on 6 Jan 1842 by Daniel Steele, Deacon of the Methodist-Episcopal Church in Pike Co., Kentucky. Pike Co., KY Marr. Rec. They were parents of 10 known children: Elizabeth, Joseph, Richard, Charity, Tandy, Adaline, Sarah Jane, Milly, Clifton & Mary Ann "Polly" Branham.
