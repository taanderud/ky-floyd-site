Willie & Haskell Hughes - mid 1930's Willie Hughes (left) and Haskell Hughes, sons of Chester & Vady (Hicks) Hughes at Lackey, KY in Floyd County. Willie (1922-1993) lived in Ashland, KY after serving in the Army. Survived by wife Alma (Craycraft) and 2 sons: Billy & Larry "Bucky" Hughes. Haskell (1920-1992) also lived in Ashland, KY after serving in the Navy. Survived by wife Deloris (maiden name unknown) and 1 son, Haskell "Hacky" Hughes Jr. 
Submitted by Billy (s/o Willie)
