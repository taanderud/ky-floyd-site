Golda Pear (Stiltner) Akers.  B: January, 1915.  She is the daughter of James Stiltner and Belle Chaney of Pike Co, KY but, lived in Floyd County most of her life. The oldest is Margie Marie Akers, B: 1934 and the youngest girl is Betty Sue (Akers) Carpenter, B: 1937.  The child on her lap is Windel Lee Akers B: 1940.
