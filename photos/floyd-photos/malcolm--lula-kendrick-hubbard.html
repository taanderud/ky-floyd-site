Floyd County Family Pictures

Photograph of my great-grandparents, Malcolm & Lula Kendrick Hubbard. Malcolm was the son of William and Sarah "Sallie" Hammons Hubbard. Malcolm was born August 23, 1871 in Floyd Co., KY. Lula was the daughter of George T. and Dicy May Kendrick. Lula was born December 30, 1881 in Floyd Co., KY. Both Malcolm and Lula died in Ohio and are buired at Fairview Cemetery in Centerpoint, OH.
