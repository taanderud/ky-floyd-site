James Bartley Click  b. 12-10-1800  d.  03-01-898 s/o John Click & Susan Drake This picture was taken in 1896 and appeared in the Cincinnati Enquirer newspaper along with a letter from William Jennings Bryan to James Click thanking him for riding his mule 6 miles to vote for him in the presidential election.  The article listed him as being 106 years old.  Some sources list his birth as 10 Dec 1790. Charles Rice
