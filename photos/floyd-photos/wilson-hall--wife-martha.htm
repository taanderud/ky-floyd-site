Wilson Henderson Hall & wife Martha Stapleton Blanton Hall
Wilson Henderson Hall s/o Esquire "Squire" Hall & Eleanor Sturgill was b. 1836  d.  05-04-1918 at Caney, Pike Co., KY.  Burial Newsom Cemetery.
Martha Stapleton Blanton Hall d/o John Stapleton & Margaret "Peggy" Salyers, was born Feb. 1846 and died 06-05-1918 Pike Co., KY. She married first William "Will" Blanton. Married second Wilson Henderson Hall on 07-25-1883 in Johnson Co., KY.
