James & Lilly May (White) Wright and some of their children. Take ca. 1916. Pictured left to right are: On fathers knee: Henry Wright, b. 7-21-1912, Floyd County Father: James Wright,   b. 4-19-1886, Floyd Co., s/o James Harrison & Seatta (Baldridge) Wright.. He died 5-9-1967 in Greenup Co. and was buried in the Warnock Cemetery. Child standing next to father: George Wright, my daddy, b.7-15-1907. Child standing next to mother: Warren Wright, b. 1-20-1910. Mother: Lilly May White, b. 9-13-1887, Floyd Co, d/o John Lee & Minerva (Vance) White. She died 5-4-1953 in Greenup Co. and was buried at the Warnock Cemetery. Child on mothers lap: Bertha Wright, b.10-22-1915.  Submitted by
