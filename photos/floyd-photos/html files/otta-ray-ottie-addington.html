Otta Ray "Ottie" Addington and her daughter, Rhoda Elliott, with Rhoda's children.  Ottie, pictured on the left wearing glasses, was born August 1892 in Floyd Co.  She was the daughter of Monroe and Sarah Delaney Addington.  Ottie was the half sister of my grandmother, Inez Addington Hubbard.
