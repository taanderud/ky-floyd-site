McKinley Cox

Born 04-05-1896, killed in WWI on 08-29-1918 was PFC US Marine Corps. s/o Florence Martin & John Cox. Grand son of Wyatt Martin & Clarisa Jones & Sarah Eddington & Frank Cox. Brother of Millie Jane Akers, Roenie Slone, Lizzie Howell, Fennie Morgan, Hubert Cox, Evelyn Caudill and Bill Cox.
