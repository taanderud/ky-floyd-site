Floyd County Photos

William A (Big Bill-Willie) Burchett And Sara (Sallie) Wells Burchett. Bill was born in Cass Co. Mo. 9-April 1886. Sallie was born at Auxier, Ky. 17 Aug. 1882. They were married approximately 1906 in Floyd, co. They had five daughters and two sons. They lived all their lives in Floyd County, on Johns creek And at Cliff. Bill died Dec 9 1962. Sallie died Jan. 11 1972. Both are buried in the George cemetery at Cliff, Ky.
