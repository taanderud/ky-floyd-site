Probably taken by the Big Sandy around 1900, give or take 10 years. We believe the older woman on the left is Rebecca Mayo Clark (1855-approx.1916). She was the w/o Lindsey Clark. We also believe the younger women might be her daughters, Anna Clark (Stephens), Lulie Clark (Crisp) & Rhoda "Bossie"  Clark (Clark). We cannot figure out who the other people are.
