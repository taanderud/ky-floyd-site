Floyd County Family Pictures

Benjamin Hall was born ca 1843, in either Pike or Letcher County. s/o Reuben Hall, Jr. & Mahala Bentley. He appeared to have previously served in the 13th Kentucky Cavalry, Company D. In the 13th, Benjamin attained the rank of 4th Sergeant and from the records appears to have hired a substitute. Thomas may have left the rebel service because on September 27, 1863, both enlisted in the 39th at Louisa. Benjamin Hall is buried in Pike County at the Joe Trivette Cemetery with a government issued headstone.
