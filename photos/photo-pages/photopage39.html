Malcolm Hubbard House

Malcolm & Lula Kendrick Hubbard with Mary Alice Hubbard Burchett and Ruth Owens Burchett. Malcolm and Mary Alice Hubbard were the children of William M. and Sarah "Sallie" Hammons Hubbard.  Lula Kendrick was the daughter of George T. and Dicy May Kendrick.  Ruth Burchett was the daughter of Floyd and Mary Alice Hubbard Burchett.  Ruth was married to James Harvey Owens.  This photograph was given to me by my cousin, Naomi Owens Day.  I believe the photograph was taken about 1945 in Ohio.

Marcus Leon Kingdom Jones He was known as King Jones. He was born 12-12-1870 and he died 5-5-1951. His father was Amrose Jones. King Jones was first married to Mary Howell and second to Sarah (Justice) Smith.
