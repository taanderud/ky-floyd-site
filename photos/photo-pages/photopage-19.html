Jonathan Watkins

Born July 16, 1849 in Magoffin County, KY. Died

March 11, 1932 in Portsmouth, OH (Scioto County) in the home of his daughter Lucy. He was the son of Sylvester

Wadkins/Watkins and Hester (Hattie) Tussey. The Grandson of Benedict Wadkins and Barbara Stanley. He was

married to Margaret Skeens on March 04, 1869 in Floyd County, KY. They had one son Thomas Watkins born

June 28, 1871 in Floyd County. After Margaret's death he was awarded guardian of their son Thomas.

After his first wife's death Jonathan married Anna Hicks on November 08, 1876 at the home of Wesley Hicks in Floyd County.

No children born of this marriage.

Jonathan married my grandmother Millie Nelson on February 21, 1901 in Greenup County, KY. They were divorced on August 09,

1922 in Antrim County, Michigan. Millie was the daughter of John Nelson and Nancy Kitchen. Jonathan and Millie had six children.

June b. July 16, 1903 d. November 09, 1975

Lucy Bell b. July 17, 1905 d. April 23, 1982

Everett b. July 27, 1908 d. September 16, 1970

James Russell b. April 08, 1911 d. June 10, 1939

William Paul b. April 18, 1913 d. May 29, 1983

another daughter, still living
