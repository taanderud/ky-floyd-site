 Josephine Campbell Wallen Hicks b. 05-06-1912 In Heller, Pike Co. KY, d. 03-19-1967 in Warsaw, Indiana. Married Joe Baker Wallen 1924. 5 children: Mary Ellen Rose,  Dewey Vernon, Joe David, Angie Bell Horn, Paul Edward. Divorced Joe 1939, Married Earl Hicks 1944. Josephine's Parents were William Zenith Campbell & Mary Allen. Josephine and her husband Earl were the proprietors of a small restaurant in W. Prestonsburg Kentucky across from the train depot before moving to Indiana sometime during the 1950.
