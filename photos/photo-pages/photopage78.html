These pictures were taken in fall of 1950, I think, in Wyandotte, MI. The boy in the uniform was Cpl. Roy Layne and his family. He was killed in Korea in April 1951, I think. He was from Betsy Layne, KY. I think his father was a minister there. I think his name was Rev. Carl Layne. I have tried to locate his family to return these pictures. I found them when we sold my mother's house. I remember they all were so nice. I really hope to find his family
