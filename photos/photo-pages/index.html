Willie Hughes, s/o Chester and Vady (Hicks) Hughes, Lackey, KY in Floyd County. Born August 6, 1922 in the Lackey, KY area. Married Alma Craycraft of Grayson, KY in Carter County and lived in Ashland, KY until his death October 14, 1993. Was the wonderful parents of 2 sons: Billy J. and Larry "Bucky" He had one older brother, Haskell, who also lived in Ashland, KY with his wife Deloris (last name unknown) and one son, Haskell "Hacky" Jr. Dad spoke very little about his family life in Floyd Co. or his time spent with the Army as a medic during World War 2.

William A (Big Bill-Willie) Burchett And Sara (Sallie) Wells Burchett. Bill was born in Cass Co. Mo. 9-April 1886. Sallie was born at Auxier, Ky. 17 Aug. 1882. They were married approximately 1906 in Floyd, co. They had five daughters and two sons. They lived all their lives in Floyd County, on Johns creek And at Cliff. Bill died Dec 9 1962. Sallie died Jan. 11 1972. Both are buried in the George cemetery at Cliff, Ky.
