Lee & Elizabeth Akers  Hamilton w/o Lee Mitchell Photo from New Salem Association of Old Regular Baptist Sept. 1987 Martha Jane Harris Clarke.  Postmistress of the Cliff, KY post office and she and my ggrandfather, William Sherman Tecumseh Clarke lived at Brandy Keg. B. 11-2-1899 in VA  D. 2-14-1954 in Floyd Co. Marriage-9-12-1888 at the home of Jefferson Davis Harris in Floyd Co. Her home was in Brandy Keg and was torn down to create the Jenny Wiley lake.  Her house was located approximately where the boat dock is currently located.  My dad remembers visiting with her while she was postmistress of Cliff, KY.  He remembers the US Mail being delivered to her by mule in big leather satchels on the mule's back.
