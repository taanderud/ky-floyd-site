  		This photo (circa 1875) is of brothers Davis Larkin (Paddy) Akers, & Hugh Parsons (Bud) Akers, born in Hunt county Texas in 1858 and 1850, respectively. s/o Claibourne & Oma Akers. Claibourne was born in Floyd Co. Ky circa 1817, s/o Solomon Akers Jr. & Matilda Meade. Claibourne Akers was the first Akers from Floyd county to migrate to Texas, arriving with his spouse Oma Parsons in 1842.
