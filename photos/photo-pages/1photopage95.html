William A (Big Bill-Willie) Burchett And Sara (Sallie) Wells Burchett. Bill was born in Cass Co. Mo. 9-April 1886. Sallie was born at Auxier, Ky. 17 Aug. 1882. They were married approximately 1906 in Floyd, co. They had five daughters and two sons. They lived all their lives in Floyd County, on Johns creek And at Cliff. Bill died Dec 9 1962. Sallie died Jan. 11 1972. Both are buried in the George cemetery at Cliff, Ky.

Submitted by Kermit Collins

 Willie & Haskell Hughes - mid 1930's Willie Hughes (left) and Haskell Hughes, sons of Chester & Vady (Hicks) Hughes at Lackey, KY in Floyd County. Willie (1922-1993) lived in Ashland, KY after serving in the Army. Survived by wife Alma (Craycraft) and 2 sons: Billy & Larry "Bucky" Hughes. Haskell (1920-1992) also lived in Ashland, KY after serving in the Navy. Survived by wife Deloris (maiden name unknown) and 1 son, Haskell "Hacky" Hughes Jr.
