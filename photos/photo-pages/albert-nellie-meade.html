Floyd County Family Pictures

Nellie Lee Compton Meade & Albert Richard "Alb" Meade at their home on Hurricane Creek at Boldman, Pike County, Kentucky. Nellie was the daughter of John Wallace Compton & Ida Belle Adkins. She was born 22 February 1915 and passed away 31 March 2001. She is buried in the Albert Meade Family Cemetery, Hurricane Creek, Pike County, Kentucky, with her husband, Alb. Alb was born 22 July 1910 in Pike County, KY. He was the son of Alexander Meade and Lindy Stephens. Alb was a farmer and a coal miner, where he was crippled in a cave-in. He died on 7 April 1981. Alb and Nellie were the parents of 8 children, Mildred, Mary, Charles, Burley, Betty, Clint, Richard and Bobby.

The Old Home Place, home of Albert and Nellie Meade, Hurricane Creek Road, Pikeville, KY 41501. It has since been torn down. The Albert Meade Family Cemetery is behind this house on the hill.

Albert Richard Meade "Alb" and Nellie Lee Compton some time before their marriage in 1932. They had been fighting and Nellie tore the picture in half. After they made up, she taped it back together!
