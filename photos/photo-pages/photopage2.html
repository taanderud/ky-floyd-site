Adrian Hubbard, Garner Hubbard, and Ernest Clark. Adrian was born about 1927 in Floyd Co.  He was the son of Mallory and Eva Hurd Hubbard.  Adrian married Mary Louise Boggs.  Garner, shown sitting in the rear, was born January 29, 1901 in Floyd Co.  He was the son of Malcolm and Lula Kendrick Hubbard. Garner Hubbard married Veda Miller.  Ernest Clark was born about 1900 and was married to Ethel Hubbard, the daughter of Malcolm and Lula Kendrick Hubbard.  This photograph was given to me by my cousin, Naomi Owens Day.

Submitted by Kathy
