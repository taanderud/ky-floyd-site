Floyd County Family Pictures

On june 18 we had the D. Emit and Virgie Hamilton family reunion in McDowell Ky.. Present were four surviving children, Stella Howell 91, Margie Parsons 84, Draxie Newsome 78, and Elmer Hamilton 76. They are living history of Frasure Creek and Floyd County. They all have very good memories.

Submitted by Jerry Howell
