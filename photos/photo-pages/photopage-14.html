Hubbard Family

Standing: Mary Alice Hubbard Burchett and Malcom Hubbard Seated L-R: William M. Hubbard, Jr. and Sarah "Sallie" Hammons Hubbard, Malory Hubbbard, Lula Kendrick with Clyde Hubbard on her lap. This photo was taken about 1905 in Floyd Co., KY and is the property of Reverend Howard and Nancy Hubbard. Mary Alice Hubbard was born Feb, 1874. She was the d/o William (Jr.) and Sarah Hammons Hubbard and the wife of Floyd Burchett. Malcom Hubbard was born 23 August 1871. He was the s/o William (Jr.) and Sarah Hammons Hubbard and he was married to Lula Kendrick. William M. Hubbard, Jr. was born 07 June 1840. He was the s/o William (Sr.) and Frances Jackson. He was married to Sarah "Sallie" Hammons. Sarah "Sallie" Hammons was born 18 August 1852. She was the d/o James and Stacy Broughton Hammons. She was married to William M. Hubbard, Jr. on 18 March 1866 in Floyd County. Malory Hubbard was born 29 September 1897. He was the s/o Malcom and Lula Kendrick Hubbard. Malory was married to Eva Hurd. Lula Kendrick was born 30 December 1881. She was the d/o Dr. George T. and Dicy Emmaline May Kendrick. Lula was married to Malcom Hubbard on 28 December 1896 in Floyd County. Clyde Hubbard was born 11 March 1905. He was the s/o Malcom and Lula Kendrick Hubbard. Clyde was married to Inez Addington on 17 October 1924 in Floyd County.

Maryetta Burchett Born 1858 on Johns Creek, Floyd county Ky. She was a daughter of Fleming and Mary Ann Perry Burchett. She married Armstead Burchett in the early 1880's moved to Main City, Cass county Mo. There They had four children. Hattie, Sally, Theodore, and William A (Big Bill). Her husband Armstead died of Typhoid fever Nov. 200'. 1886. She then returned to Floyd county with her four children. In 1889 She married Jake Setser, and of this union there were two more daughters Josephine, and Mintie. She lived ,the latter years with Her daughter Josephine. She lived 86 years of her life in Floyd county. She passed away at 90 yrs of age in 1948 in Prestonsburg, Ky. She was My Great Grandmother ------Kermit Collins.
